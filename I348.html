Mary Jane Dale (née Downey) 1857 - 1897

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Mary Downey, the wife of Edward Dale (the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in South Shields, Tyne and Wear, England in Jul 18571,2,3,4,5,6 and married Edward (a coal porter with whom she had 3 children: Agnes, Leonard Arthur and Sidney Albert) in South Shields, Durham, England on Jun 5, 18777.

Throughout her life, Mary lived in several places: in Westoe, South Tyneside, England in 18611 and in 18712; in Milton, Gravesend, Kent, England in 18813; at Cottage, Milton in Gravesend on Apr 3, 18818; and in Milton in 18914.

She died in Oct 1897 in Kent6.
