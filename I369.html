Hannah Mary Baldock (née Knowler) 1889 - c1945

The daughter of Thomas Knowler (an agricultural laborer) and Jane LawrenceHannah Knowler, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ash, Kent, England on Jun 17, 18891,2,3,4,5, was baptized there at St Nicholas on Oct 5, 1890 and married Henry Baldock (a farm laborer with whom she had 2 children: Winifred Alice and William James) in Eastry, Kent, England around Nov 19077 (Oct/Nov/Dec).

Hannah spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: in Staple, Kent, England on Apr 5, 18911; in Woodnesborough, Kent, England on Mar 31, 19012; in Barnsole, Kent, England on Apr 2, 19119 (the same place as her father had been living on Apr 2, 1871); in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Jun 19, 192110; and at 2 Handville Green Cottages, Bridge on Sep 29, 19398.

She died c. Feb 1945 in Canterbury, Kent, England4,6.
