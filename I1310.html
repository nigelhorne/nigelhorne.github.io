Jane Eliza Caroline Fuller (née Tutt) 1876 - 1945

A general servant and the daughter of James Tutt (a general laborer) and Jane Foord (a general shop)Jane Tutt, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on Sep 13, 18761,2 and married Mark Fuller (a general laborer with whom she had 5 children: Mark Anthony, Kate Rosaline, James Richard Edmund, Charles Thomas and Elizabeth Rebecca) in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 3, 18992.

Throughout her life, Jane lived in several places: at 18 Clarence Terrace, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 3, 18815, when she was living with her aunt, Jane grandmother, Caroline Bromley; on Clarence Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 5, 18914 - less than a mile from her cousin on her father's side Walter Tunbridge and aunt Eliza Tutt who were living at 9 Elms Cottage, Hougham, Kent; on 22 Central Road, Ramsgate, Kent on Apr 2, 19113 - less than a mile from her father James who was living on 7 Hillbrow Road, St Lawrence in Thanet; and on 3 Central Road, Ramsgate, Kent on Jun 19, 19216.

She died on Jun 4, 1945 at 87 Rose Avenue, Toronto, Ontario, Canada and was buried at Prospect Cemetery, Toronto on Jun 7, 1945 (a day with light rain).
