The child of James Lawrence (a general farm worker) and Mary Knowler, George Lawrence, the first cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Haine, Kent, England on 4 Feb 19201,2,3,4, was baptised in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 25 Apr 1920 and married Margaret Collins (with whom he had 5 children: Brian George, Valerie Anne, James, Robin and John Edward, along with 4 surviving children) in Thanet, Kent, England around May 19405 (Apr/May/Jun).

During his life, he was living at Meadow Cottages, Marshside, Kent on 19 Jun 19211; at 61 Percy Avenue, Kingsgate, Kent in 195311, in 195510, in 19639 and in 19658; and at 146 Newington Road, Ramsgate, Kent in 19717, in 19746 and in 1976.

He died on 25 Sept 1976 in Ramsgate, Kent, England3.
