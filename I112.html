A general farm worker and the child of James Lawrence (an agricultural labourer) and Elizabeth Mount (a domestic servant), James Lawrence, the great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 4 Oct 18931,2,3,4 and married his cousin on his father's side Mary Knowler (with whom he had 5 children: James Thomas, George Edward, Thomas William, Jack and Ronald) at St Augustine, Northbourne, Kent, England on 14 Sept 19185 (JulAug/Sep).

During his life, he was living in Staple, Kent, England on 31 Mar 19011; at Oast Cottages, Cleve, Monkton, Kent on 2 Apr 19116 - less than a mile from his half-sister Ethel May who was living at 2 Oast Cottage, Cleve, Monkton, Kent and his half-sister Florence Eva who was living at 2 Oast Cottages, Cleve, Monkton, Kent -; at Meadow Cottages, Marshside, Kent on 19 Jun 19218; and at 1 Church Cottage, Woodnesborough, Kent on 29 Sept 19392. He died on 20 Jan 1956 in Ashford, Kent, England3 (died running for the bus. He had had a heart attack. [ No citation for evidence ]) and was buried at Ramsgate Cemetery, Ramsgate, Kent after 20 Jan 1956.
