The child of Alexander Bowie and Emily Cock, Wilfred Bowie, the second cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Edinburgh, Midlothian, Scotland on 4 Apr 1920. He was married to Joyce Evelyn Ross.He served in the military from 18 Mar 1940 to 16 Nov 1945 (sergeant RAAF, service number 26032). In 1927 he travelled from London, England (s. S. Baradine) to Australia, departing on 1 Sep, arriving later that same year (s. S. Baradine.).

He died on 21 Feb 2006 in Adelaide, South Australia, Australia and was buried at Centennial Park Cemetery, Pasadena, Mitcham, South Australia after 21 Feb 20061.
