Stanley Wallace Page 1887 - 1917

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The son of James Page and Elizabeth Sellis, Stanley Page, the third cousin once-removed on the father's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England on May 13, 1887<span class="citation">1</span>, was baptized there at St John The Baptist on Aug 28, 1887. He married Beatrice Smith (with whom he had 1 surviving child) in Elham, Kent, England around Nov 1910<span class="citation">5</span>. In 1915, he lived on 113A Canterbury Road in Folkestone<span class="citation">7</span>. In 1917 he was serving in the military in Iraq (<em>soldier Number: 121686, Rank: Gunner, Corps: Royal Garrison Artillery</em>). <p>He died on Jul 24, 1917 in Baghdad, Iraq<span class="citation">2,3,4</span> (heat Stroke) and was buried there at Baghdad War Cemetery (North Gate) after Jul 24, 1917<span class="citation">6</span>.
