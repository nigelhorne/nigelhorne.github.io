A mariner and the child of William Cowell and Elizabeth Wales, William Cowell, the first cousin four-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 10 Feb 18111, was baptised there on 17 Mar 1811 and also married Sarah Pettit (with whom he had 2 children: Sarah Ann and Susannah Elizabeth) there on 25 Dec 1855.

During his life, he was living at 1 Nelson Place, St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 7 Apr 18615 and on 2 Apr 18714. He served in the navy from 1833 to 1855 (Royal Navy).

He died c. May 1877 in Thanet2 and was buried in St Peters on 7 Jun 18773.
