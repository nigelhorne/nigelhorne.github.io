Ann M Serjeant (née Mount) c1784 - 1861

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

The child of John Mount and Margaret Lamb, Ann Mount, the five times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Kent, England c. 17841, was baptised in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on 23 Dec 1787 and married James Serjeant (with whom she had 5 children: Thomas, John, Frances Elizabeth, James Edward and Charlotte) in Chislet, Kent, England on 10 Nov 18061 (Recorded at St. Nicholas). She arrived in New York, New York, USA on 14 May 1841 with her husband James (arrived from London on the Wellington).

She died on 29 Apr 1861 in Allen, Indiana, USA.
