George Thomas Knowler 1901 - 1981

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A farm horseman and the child of Thomas Knowler (an agricultural labourer) and Jane Lawrence, George Knowler, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Woodnesborough, Kent, England on 2 Sept 19011,2,3,4,5 and married Amelia Spelman (with whom he had 5 children: Patrick, Gladys Kathleen M, George Thomas Patrick, Anthony C E and Richard Henry J) in Birchington, Kent, England on 3 Oct 19256 (Our Lady and St Benedict).

During his life, he was living at Shingleton Cottages, Eastry, Kent on 2 Apr 19111; and at 2 Malthouse Cottages, Mongeham Road, Great Mongeham, Kent on 29 Sept 19392.

He died on 16 Oct 1981 at Ramsgate General Hospital, West Cliff Road, Ramsgate, Kent4.
