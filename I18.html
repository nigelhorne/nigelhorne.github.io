Emily Jane Cowell (née Lawrence) 1887 - 1950

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The daughter of James Lawrence (an agricultural labourer) and Elizabeth Mount (a domestic servant)Emily Lawrence, the grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 30 Sept 18871,2,3,4,5,6,7, was baptised there on 13 Nov 1887 and married James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company with whom she had 9 children: Frederick James, Charles John, Emily Edith, Henry Richard Stephen, Reginald Albert, James Sidney, Dorothy Helen, Freda Violet and Joyce Margaret) at Thanet Registry Office, Thanet, Kent, England on 7 Jan 190910 (Married at Thanet Registry Office). She also had 1 child, Lilian Florence, whose father is unknown.

Emily spent all of her life in Kent in England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at 10 Claremont Gardens, Ramsgate on 5 Apr 189112; in Staple, Kent, England on 31 Mar 190113; on 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on 2 Apr 191111; and at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on 19 Jun 19214 and on 29 Sept 19395.

She died on 19 Aug 1950 Minster in Thanet7,8,9 and was buried there at Minster Cemetery, Tothill St on 23 Aug 1950 (a rainy day) following her funeral at The Salvation Army Hall, 11 Tothill St7.
