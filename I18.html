Emily Jane Cowell (née Lawrence) 1887 - 1950

The child of James Lawrence (an agricultural laborer) and Elizabeth Mount (a domestic servant), Emily Lawrence, the grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Sep 30, 18871,2,3,4,5,6,7, was baptized there on Nov 13, 1887 and married James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company with whom she had 9 children: Frederick James, Charles John, Emily Edith, Henry Richard Stephen, Reginald Albert, James Sidney, Dorothy Helen, Freda Violet and Joyce Margaret) at Thanet Registry Office, Thanet, Kent, England on Jan 7, 19099 (Married at Thanet Registry Office). She also had 1 child, Lilian Florence, whose father is unknown.

During her life, she was living at 10 Claremont Gardens, Ramsgate, Kent on Apr 5, 189111; in Staple, Kent, England on Mar 31, 190112; at 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 191110; and at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Jun 19, 19214 and on Sep 29, 19395.

She died on Aug 19, 1950 Minster in Thanet7,8 and was buried there at Minster Cemetery, Tothill St after Aug 19, 19507.
