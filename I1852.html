A to shoe manufacturers clerk and elctrical clerk and the child of Thomas Horne (a brewer's drayman) and Harriett Woods, Hilda Horne, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Kings Cross, London, England on 27 Jun 18981,2,3 and baptised in Camden, London, England on 17 Jul 1898. She married Bertram Nott (a general labourer) in Paddington, London, England around Feb 19404.

During her life, she was living at Britannia Street, St Pancras, London on 31 Mar 19016; at Derby Buildings, St Pancras, London on 2 Apr 19117; at 25 Derby Buildings, St Pancras, London on 19 Jun 19211; at 19 Grittleton Road, Maida Vale, London on 29 Sept 19392, when she was living with her brother John Christopher and sister Rosa Emily; and at 130 Oxford Gardens in Paddington in 19625. In 1921 she was working at Lilley & Skinner in Kings Cross, London, England.

She died c. Nov 1981 in Kensington, London, England3.
