The child of William Adams and Sarah Woodruff, Mildred Adams, the 5 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England c. 17521 and married John Culmer (a labourer with whom she had 2 children: John and Charles) in Ash, Kent, England on 27 Mar 17791.

She died in 1807 in Kent1 and was buried in Stourmouth on 8 Feb 18072.
