The child of Thomas Mount (an agricultural laborer) and Mary BuxeyElizabeth Mount, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England c. 17991,2,3,4 and baptized there on Feb 24, 1799. She married William Fairbrass (an agricultural laborer) in Sturry, Kent, England on Aug 22, 1819.
