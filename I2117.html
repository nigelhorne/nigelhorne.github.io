Harriet Boodle (née Culmer) c1830 - c1904

The child of John Culmer (an agricultural labourer) and Phoebe Port, Harriet Culmer, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Hoath, Kent, England c. 18301,2, was baptised there on 31 Oct 1830 and married Benjamin Boodle (an agricultural labourer with whom she had 12 children: Francis, George, John, Ann, Edward, Henry, Mary, William, Jane, Esther, Thomas and Alfred, along with 1 surviving child) at Ss Cosmus & Damian's Church, Blean, Kent, England on 29 Oct 18484.

During her life, she was living at Clapham Hill in Blean on 30 Mar 18511; at New Road in Blean on 7 Apr 18615 (when she was living with her); on Fordwich Lane, Canterbury, Kent on 2 Apr 18716; in Blean on 3 Apr 18817 (the same place as her parents had been living on 6 Jun 1841); and at Plough Row, Harbledown, Kent on 31 Mar 19012.

She died c. May 1904 in Bridge, Kent, England3.
