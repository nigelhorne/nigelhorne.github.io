A fish carrier and the child of Henry Culmer (a coal carman) and Eliza Arnold, Charles Culmer, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Preston, Wingham, Kent, England on 23 Nov 18731,2, was baptised there on 11 Jan 1874 and married Elizabeth Hook (with whom he had 2 children: Charles Frederick and May Ellen Elizabeth) in St. Mary Cray, Kent, England on 27 Aug 18933.

During his life, he was living on Farwig Lane, Bromley, Kent on 31 Mar 19014; and in Hallowell, Prince Edward, Ontario, Canada in 19115 which is where he died on 6 Oct 1936. He arrived in Canada in 1906.He was buried at Glenwood Cemetery Company, Picton, Ontario on 8 Oct 1936.
