A laborer and the son of Troward Cowell (an agricultural laborer) and Mary CastleJohn Cowell, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Wingham, Kent, England in Jul 18431,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 and married Caroline Twyman (with whom he had 6 children: George William, Charlotte Priscilla Ann, Henry John, Emily Jane, Frederick Troward and Annie Mary, along with 1 surviving child) at St Andrews, Wickhambreaux, Kent, England on Aug 30, 186210.

John spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at Old Farm House in Wingham on Mar 30, 18511; at Wingham Street in Wingham on Apr 7, 18612; on London Road, Ditton on Apr 2, 187112; on Orchard Terrace, New Hythe Road, East Malling on Apr 3, 188111; on Lunsford Lane, East Malling on Apr 5, 189113; in East Malling, Kent, England in 19016; and at The Banks, Larkfield on Apr 2, 191114.

He died in Jun 1920 in Malling, Kent, England8.
