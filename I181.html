Ann Ralph, the wife of Henry Coleman (the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne), was born in Sarre, Kent, England on Aug 23, 18071,2 and baptized in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Sep 20, 1807. She married Henry (a gardener and domestic) there, on Sep 23, 18493 (St Nicholas Parish Church).

Ann spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived at Monkton Row, Monkton, Kent, England on Jun 6, 18416; and at her birthplace in 1851, on Apr 7, 18615 and on Apr 2, 18714.

She died on Nov 24, 1880 in Thanet, Kent, England and was buried in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Dec 1, 1880.
