The child of William Bedingfield and Mary Brawner, Clement Bedingfield, the 8 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Wootton, Kent, England c. 1643, was baptised there on 24 Mar 1643 and married Margaret Twyman (with whom he had 1 child, Alice) in Margate, Kent, England on 2 Nov 16763 (St. John's the Baptist).

He died c. 1715 in Margate1,2 and was buried there at St John the Baptist Church on 6 Jan 1716 (St. John's the Baptist)1,3.
