The child of Richard Myers and Ann, Maven Myers was born in North Shields, Tyne and Wear, England in 18331, was baptized in Tynemouth, Tyne and Wear, England on Mar 19, 1833 and married Mary Gray (with whom he had 7 children: Henry, Alice Gray, Isabella, Maven, William, Emeline and Harriett A) at All Saints, Wigan, Lancashire, England on Aug 6, 18552. Like his father, he was a coal miner.

Throughout his life, Maven lived in several places: at Percy Main, Chirton, Tyne and Wear, England on Jun 6, 18413; at Victoria Inn, South Shields, Tyne and Wear on Mar 30, 18511; and in Lackawanna, Pennsylvania, USA on Jun 1, 18804 and on Jun 1, 19005. In 1857 he traveled from Liverpool, Merseyside, England (on the ship Webster) to New York, New York, USA, arriving on 24 Oct.

He died in 1901 in Pennsylvania.
