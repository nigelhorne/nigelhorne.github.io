The child of Richard Myers and AnnMaven Myers was born in North Shields, Tyne and Wear, England in 18331, was baptised in Tynemouth, Tyne and Wear, England on 19 Mar 1833 and married Mary Gray (with whom he had 7 children: Henry, Alice Gray, Isabella, Maven, William, Emeline and Harriett A) at All Saints, Wigan, Lancashire, England on 6 Aug 18552.
