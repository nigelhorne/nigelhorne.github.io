The child of John Marion (a confectioner: 254 king street: importer of french confectionary and toys) and Ellen Haily, John Marion, the first cousin four-times-removed on the father's side of Michele Copp (née Phillips), was born in South Carolina c. 18431. In the Georgetown University Archives there is a poem written by John Francis Marion while a student there on Jan 1, 1855. Attended Georgetown College (Georgetown University today) - 6 year course - major in Poetry. Had to leave 90 days before graduation becuase of start of civil war on Jan 1, 1855.

He died on Jun 26, 1864 in Jackson Hospital, Richmond, VA (typhoid fever on Battlefield).
