William Lawrence c1827 - 1898

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A harbor laborer and the child of Zechariah Lawrence (a laborer) and Ann Cooper, William Lawrence, the great-great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Word in Worth, Sandwich, Kent, England c. 18271,2,3,4,5, was baptized in Woodnesborough, Kent, England on Aug 5, 1827 and was orphaned in 1833 by the death of both parents in that year her.

He married Johanna Edwards (with whom he had 11 children: George, Mary Carter, William, John, Annie, Elizabeth, Daniel, James, Hannah, Jane and Emily, along with 2 surviving children) in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Nov 22, 18517

William spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Preston in 1841; on Chapel Lane, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 7, 18619; at Rear of Southwood Lodge, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 187110; and at 3 Providence Cottage, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 5, 18914. William appeared in East Kent Times and Mail on 22 Jun 1898

He died on Jun 11, 1898 at 1 Pear Tree Cottages, St Lawrence in Thanet5,6 (morbis Cordis) and was buried there on Jun 15, 18988.
