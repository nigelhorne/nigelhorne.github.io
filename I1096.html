William Ernest Standing 1878 - 1969

A laundry man and the 3rd of 4 children of William Standing (a laundry keeper) and Adeliza Burnham, William Standing, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England on 12 Aug 18781,2,3, was baptised in Christ Church, Folkestone, Kent, England on 22 Sept 1878 went to Yerbury Road School, Islington, London, England He married Eliza Parr (with whom he had 7 children: Victor William, Ernest Douglas, Stephen Earling, Muriel Alice, Stephen Lawrence, Bernard Walter and Brenda Margaret) in Ilford, London, England on 29 Aug 19144.

During his life, he was living at York Rise, Highgate, London on 3 Apr 18818; at Edmund Street, Kettering, Northamptonshire, England on 5 Apr 18919; at Lansdowne Road, Charlton, London on 31 Mar 190110; at 5 Aurelia Cottages, Ley Street in Ilford on 2 Apr 19117, in 19256 and on 29 Sept 19391; and in Ilford in 19645 which is where he died on 13 Sept 19693. During 1917, he was serving in the military in Norfolk, England (RAF - service number 80614).He was buried there at Barkingside Garden of Rest, Longwood Gardens after 13 Sept 1969.
