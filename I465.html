A wholesale fruiterer and the child of John Spain (a laborer) and Anne SuttonJames Spain, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England in 18271,2,3,4, was baptized there on Dec 23, 1827 and married Sarah Cornwell (with whom he had 1 child, Sarah Ann) at St Mildred's Church, Canterbury, Kent, England on Jan 18, 18536.

James spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Canterbury in 18611; at Cossington Street in Canterbury on Apr 2, 187110; and on 32 Old Dover Road in Canterbury on Apr 3, 18819, on Apr 5, 18918 and in 1895 which is where he died on Nov 235 (fell off ladder). He was buried at St Martin, Canterbury on Nov 28, 18957.
