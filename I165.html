The child of Edward Dale (an agricultural labourer) and Rebecca Crockford, Sophia Dale, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Chartham, Kent, England in Oct 18401,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10, was baptised there on 11 Oct 1840 and also married Thomas Epps (a labourer on a farm with whom she had 5 children: Eliza, Stephen, Emma, Emily and Edward, along with 1 surviving child) there at St Mary on 24 Dec 185612.

During her life, she was living in Chartham Hatch, Kent, England on 6 Jun 184117 and on 30 Mar 185115; in Thanington, Kent, England in 18613; at Dog Kennel House in Chartham on 2 Apr 187118; at her birthplace in 18815 (the same place as her mother had been living in 1871); at Milton Cottage in Chartham on 5 Apr 189116 (when she was living with her); and at Marlowe Terrace in Thanington on 31 Mar 190114.
