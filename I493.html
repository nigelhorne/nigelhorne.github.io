The child of Samuel Bridgman and Mary VentimanRachel Bridgman, the 8 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Petham, Kent, England in 1619, was baptized there on Jan 1, 1620 and married William Newport (with whom she had 7 children: Susan, John, William, Elizabeth, Thomas, Sarah and Mary) in St Mildreds, Canterbury, Kent, England on Jan 25, 16431.

She died in 1690 in Waltham, Kent, England.
