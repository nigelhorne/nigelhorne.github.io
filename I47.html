Sarah Spratt (née Spain) c1814 - 1884

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of John Spain (a laborer) and Susanna NewportSarah Spain, the great-great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England c. 18141,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptized there on Oct 23, 1814 and married James Spratt (an agricultural laborer with whom she had 9 children: Eliza, Mary, Emma, Jane, James Henry, John, Charles, William and Alfred, along with 3 surviving children) in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Jan 9, 18429 (Parish Church/St. Nicholas At Wade, Kent, England).

Sarah spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Crump's Farm in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Jun 6, 184114 and on Mar 30, 185113; at The Row in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 7, 186112; at Parish Cottage in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 2, 187116; and at The Row in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 3, 188115.

She died on Jun 7, 1884 in St Nicholas-at-Wade6,7 (bronchitis) and was buried there on Jun 13, 1884 (aged 69 yrs)10,11.
