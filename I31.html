The son of James Spratt and Sarah SpainJohn Spratt, the great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Mar 26, 18521,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptized there on Apr 25, 1852 and married Sophia Dale (with whom he had 11 children: Leonard, Ada, Ernest, Lillian, May, Harry Dale, George Dale, Ida, Hilda, Ella and Reuben) at St Mary's Church, Chartham, Kent, England on Nov 9, 187611 (Rev Randolph, St Marys). He also had 1 child, Amy, whose mother is unknown. Like his father, he was an agricultural laborer.

John spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at The Row in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 7, 186115; in Reculver, Kent, England in 18712; at his birthplace on Nov 9, 1876; at The Street in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 3, 188114; at Shuart Cottage, Podden Street in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 5, 189116; on 2 Nash Court Cottage, Nash Road, Margate on Mar 31, 190112, when he was living with his daughter May following the death of his wife on Jan 6, 1940; on Nash Court Cottage, Nash Road, Margate on Apr 2, 191113; and at Nash, Thanet on Jun 19, 19217. He died on Aug 4, 1928 on 2 Hill View, Twenties, Shottendane Road, Margate, Kent9,10 (cachexia; Stomach Cancer).
