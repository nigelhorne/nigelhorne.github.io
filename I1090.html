Thomas Chandler, the husband of Mary Culmer (the seven times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Ash, Kent, England c. 1717 and baptised there on 8 Dec 1717. He married Mary there at St Nicholas Church on 21 Oct 17451, which is also where he died on 25 Oct 1788.
