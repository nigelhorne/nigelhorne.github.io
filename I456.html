Ellen Elizabeth Dale (née Smith) 1869 - 1959

Ellen Smith, the wife of Leonard Dale (the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Swalecliffe, Kent, England on Nov 14, 18691,2,3,4,5 and married Leonard (a boiler attendant brickworks with whom she had 2 children: George and Edith Kate, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mary's Church, Chartham, Kent, England on Mar 14, 18968.

Throughout her life, Ellen lived in several places: in Ivychurch, Kent, England in 18811; at 88 Church Hill, Temple Ewell, Kent on Mar 31, 190111; on 12 Douglas Road, Dover, Kent on Apr 2, 191110, when she was living with her daughter Edith Kate following the death of her husband on Apr 22, 1962; on Widred Road, Dover, Kent in 19219 and on Jun 19, 192112; and at Fern Cottage, Uckfield, Sussex, England on Sep 29, 19394.

She died on Oct 14, 1959 in Danehill, Sussex, England6,7.
