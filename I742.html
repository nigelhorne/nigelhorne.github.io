Charlotte Standing (née Marsh) 1793 - 1862

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Census Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Richard Marsh and Mary Bass, Charlotte Marsh, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Elham, Kent, England in 17931,2, was baptized there on Oct 6, 1793 and married William Standing (an agricultural laborer with whom she had 11 children: James, John, Mary Ann, William, Stephen, Charlotte, William, George, William Henry, Charlotte Ann and William) in Folkestone, Kent, England on Sep 29, 18134.

During her life, she was living at Lime Kiln Cottage, Dover Road in Folkestone on Jun 6, 18416; in Folkestone in 18511; and at Dover Road in Folkestone on Mar 30, 18515 and on Apr 7, 18617.

She died on Dec 13, 1862 at Canterbury Road in Folkestone3 (apoplexy) and was buried at Cheriton Road Cemetery, Folkestone on Dec 19, 1862.
