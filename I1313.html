Elizabeth Rebecca Carter (née Fuller) 1907 - 1960

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Mark Fuller (a general labourer) and Jane Tutt (a general servant), Elizabeth Fuller, the second cousin once-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England on 11 Apr 19071, was baptised there at St Luke's Church on 12 Jun 1907 and married Edwin Carter (with whom she had 3 children: Beverley Joyce, Carrollynn Winona and Kaye) in Toronto, Ontario, Canada on 15 Oct 1927.

During her life, she was living at her birthplace on 2 Apr 19112, at Central Road in Ramsgate on 19 Jun 19213, and at 3 Central Road in Ramsgate in 1925 (the same place as her parents had been living on 19 Jun 1921). In 1925 she travelled from Liverpool, Merseyside, England (cunard ship RMS Aurania) to Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada, departing on 21 Mar, arriving on 29 Mar.

She died on 27 Apr 1960 in Toronto and was buried there at Park Lawn Cemetery on 29 Apr 1960 (an overcast day).
