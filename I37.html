Charles Percy Cowell 1879 - 1967

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A newsagent and cowman and the son of John Cowell (a farm waggoner) and Emma Culmer, Charles Cowell, the great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Westbere, Kent, England on Mar 9, 18791,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there at All Saints on May 4, 1879 and married Elizabeth Russell (with whom he had 4 children: Charles John, James Henry Norman, Elizabeth Emma and Albert Percy) at All Saints Church, Birchington, Kent, England on Oct 29, 19018.

Throughout his life, Charles lived in several places: in Wayborough, Minster, Thanet, Kent on Apr 3, 18811; in Upstreet, Kent on Apr 5, 189110; at 2 Woodchurch Farm Cottages, Acol, Kent on Mar 31, 190111; at Streele Cottages, Billingshurst, West Sussex, England on Apr 2, 191112; on Full Cottages, Highfield Road, Bognor, West Sussex on Jun 19, 19214; and on Saint Stephen, Murina Avenue, Bognor Regis, West Sussex on Sep 29, 19395. Charles In 1921 he was working at A T Collgar Farmer.

He died in 1967 in Portsmouth, Hampshire, England7.
