The 3rd of 4 children of Kirby Witherden and Sarah Beake, Sampson Witherden, the first cousin six-times-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born on 14 Nov 1762 and baptised in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 5 Dec 1762. He married Mary Sheppard there on 5 Feb 1785.

He died c. 1829 (40 years sexton of this parish) and was buried in St Peters on 2 Aug 18291.
