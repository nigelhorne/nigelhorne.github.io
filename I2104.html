Christopher Gerald Jordan 1891 - 1965

The son of James Jordan (a horse keeper) and Julia Culmer, Christopher Jordan, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Faversham, Kent, England on Feb 18, 1891<span class="citation">1</span>, was baptized there on Mar 20, 1891 and married Elizabeth Moore (with whom he had 1 child, Chris Edouard) in New Zealand in 1916<span class="citation">3</span>.</p><p>Throughout his life, he lived at 2 Mill Place in Faversham on Apr 5, 1891<span class="citation">5</span> and on Mar 31, 1901<span class="citation">6</span>; and at 20 Hutchinson Avenue, New Lynn, Auckland, New Zealand in 1928<span class="citation">4</span>. On Jul 17, 1917 he was serving in the military. In 1937 he traveled from Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada to Honolulu, Hawaii, USA, arriving on 14 Jul.<p>He died on Nov 23, 1965 in Auckland, Auckland<span class="citation">2</span> and was buried there at Waikumete Cemetery after Nov 23, 1965.
