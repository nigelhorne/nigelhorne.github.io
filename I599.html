The child of John Culmer (a parish clerk) and Sarah Hawkes, Henry Culmer, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England c. 18201,2,3,4,5 and baptised there on 17 Dec 1820. He was married to Ann Jane.

During his life, he was living at his birthplace in 18511 (the same place as his mother had been living in 1841), in 18612, in 18713, in 18814 and in 18915.

He died in Jan/Feb/Mar 1896 in Eastry, Kent, England.
