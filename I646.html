Jane Hawkes (née May) 1675 - 1740

The child of Thomas May and Anne Cheesman, Jane May, the 7 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Brenchley, Kent, England in 1675, was baptised there on 26 Jul 1678 and married Luke Hawkes (with whom she had 5 children: Elizabeth, Edward, John, Luke and William) in Fordwich, Kent, England on 30 Oct 17091, which is also where she died on 23 Nov 1740.
