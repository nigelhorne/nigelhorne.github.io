The child of Thomas May and Anne CheesmanJane May, the 7 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Brenchley, Kent, England in 1675, was baptized there on Jul 26, 1678 and married Luke Hawkes (with whom she had 5 children: Elizabeth, Edward, John, Luke and William) in Fordwich, Kent, England on Oct 30, 17091, which is also where she died on Nov 23, 1740.
