An agricultural labourer and the child of Alfred Cowell (a transport driver) and Fanny Austen, Alfred Cowell, (also known as Chib Cowell) the first cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on 24 Sept 19131,2,3,4,5 and. He was married twice - to Ada Milne (on 13 Jun 1942 in Minster) Mary Williams (c. Feb 1951 in Thanet, Kent, England)6. He has 2 surviving children, both with Mary Williams: Nina J and Richard.

During his life, he was living at Durlock Farm Cottages, Minster in Thanet on 19 Jun 19211, in 1933 (the same place as his parents had been living on 19 Jun 1921), on 29 Sept 19392 and in 1942; and at 6 Edgar Road, Minster in Thanet in 19658.

He died on 17 Jan 1986 in Thanet4 and was buried on Minster Cemetery, Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet in Jan 19867.
