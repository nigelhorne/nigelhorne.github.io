A to shoe manufacturers clerk and elctrical clerk and the child of Thomas Horne (a brewer's drayman) and Harriett Woods, Hilda Horne, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Kings Cross, London, England on Jun 27, 18981,2 and baptized in Camden, London, England on Jul 17, 1898. She married Bertram Nott (a general laborer) in Paddington, London, England around Feb 19403.

During her life, she was living at Britannia Street, St Pancras, London on Mar 31, 19015; at Derby Buildings, St Pancras, London on Apr 2, 19116 and on Jun 19, 19217; at 19 Grittleton Road, Maida Vale, London on Sep 29, 19391 (when she was living with her brother John Christopher and sister Rosa Emily); and at 130 Oxford Gardens in Paddington in 19624. In 1921 she was working at Lilley & Skinner.

She died c. Nov 1981 in Kensington, London, England2.
