A farm lorry driver and the child of Henry Beake (a waggoner on a farm) and Ellen Gibbs, Alfred Beake, the second cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 19 Aug 19031,2,3 and baptised there on 1 Nov 1903. He married Doris Revell in Selling, Kent, England on 22 Mar 1952.

During his life, he was living at Hale Cottages, The Street, St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent on 2 Apr 19114; at Down Barton Farm, St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent on 19 Jun 19215, on 29 Sept 19391 (the same place as his parents had been living on 19 Jun 1921) and on 26 Apr 1941; and at 15 South Bush Lane, Rainham, Kent in 1989. In 1921 he was working at William Smith Farmer at Down Barton Farm, St Nicholas at Wade, Kent, England.

He died on 30 Oct 1989 in Rainham, Kent, England3.
