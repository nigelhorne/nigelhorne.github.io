A labourer and the child of Thomas Mount (an agricultural labourer) and Mary Buxey, Thomas Mount, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Sturry, Kent, England c. 18141, was baptised there at St Nicholas' Church on 13 Mar 1814 and married Sarah Holness (a charwoman with whom he had 10 children: Jane, Mary, Thomas, Elizabeth, William, Caroline, James, Alfred, Sarah Ann and Alice) at St Martin's Church, Herne, Kent, England on 14 Jul 18383.

During his life, he was living at Mortimer Street, Herne Bay, Kent on 30 Mar 18516; at King Street, Herne Bay, Kent on 7 Apr 18611 and on 2 Apr 18714; and on Salts Lane, Herne Bay, Kent on 3 Apr 18815.

He died c. Aug 1883 in Blean, Kent, England2.
