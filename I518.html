The child of William Impett and Mary Howe, Sarah Impett, the 4 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Monkton, Kent, England c. 1765, was baptized there at St Mary Magdalene Church on Oct 13, 1765 and married James Spratt (with whom she had 1 child, William) St Mary Magdalene in Monkton on Oct 15, 17861 (www.fadedgenes.co.uk).

She died c. 1820 in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England and was buried there on Oct 23, 18202.
