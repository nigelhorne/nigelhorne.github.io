A mariner and the child of Isaac Stuple and Ann MountAbraham Mount, the great-great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England c. 18271,2,3,4, was baptized there on Aug 26, 1827 and married Mary Colyer (a charwoman with whom he had 5 children: William, Elizabeth Ann, Edwin Henry, Mary A and Walter Charles, along with 1 surviving child) in Herne Bay, Kent, England on Oct 22, 18595 (Registry office).

Throughout his life, Abraham lived in several places: on Salts Lane in Herne Bay on Apr 2, 18716; in Eddington, Kent, England on Apr 3, 18817; and at 2 Salts Lane in Herne Bay on Apr 5, 18913.

He died on May 12, 1897 Herne Workhouse in Herne4 (chronic cystitis, mortis paraplegia, mortis exhaustion).
