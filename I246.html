Sarah Tutt (née Peters) c1781 - 1861

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A nurse and the child of Henry Peters and Mary Scott, Sarah Peters, the 3 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England c. 17811,2,3, was baptised there at Parish Church of St Mary & St Eanswythe on 14 Apr 1782 and also married James Tutt (a farm bailiff with whom she had 4 children: James, Edmund, Elizabeth and Maria) there on 3 Oct 18015.

During her life, she was living in Cheriton, Kent, England on 6 Jun 18411; at 5 Shortlands Farm, Beckenham, Kent on 30 Mar 18517; and at Horn Street in Cheriton on 7 Apr 18613, when she was living with her daughter Maria, following the death of her husband on 18 Apr 1856.

She died in Oct/Nov 1861 in Cheriton4 and was buried there on 10 Nov 18616.
