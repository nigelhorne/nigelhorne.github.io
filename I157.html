Elizabeth Ann Horn (née Warner) 1826 - 1915

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A general shopkeeper and the daughter of Isaac Warner (a laborer) and Hannah Nethersoll, Elizabeth Warner, the great-great-grandmother of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on Sep 29, 1826<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6</span>, was baptized there on Oct 29, 1826 and married Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff with whom she had 9 children: <a href="I207.html">Elizabeth Alice</a>, <a href="I12.html">Charles</a>, <a href="I198.html">Thomas Warner</a>, <a href="I201.html">Caroline</a>, <a href="I206.html">Emily</a>, <a href="I205.html">Hannah</a>, <a href="I204.html">Harry</a>, <a href="I203.html">Isaac</a> and <a href="I202.html">Ellen Mary Ann</a>, along with 1 surviving child) at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent, England on Oct 13, 1849<span class="citation">7</span>.</p><p>Elizabeth spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at her birthplace on Jun 6, 1841; at Bromstone, St Peters, Thanet on Mar 30, 1851<span class="citation">8</span> and on Apr 7, 1861; at Northdown in Margate on Apr 2, 1871<span class="citation">9</span>; on Buddles Farm, Dane Court Road, St Peters, Thanet on Apr 3, 1881<span class="citation">10</span>; at 194 Reading Street, St Peters, Thanet on Apr 5, 1891<span class="citation">11</span>; at 1 Welbeck Terrace, Reading Street, St Peters, Thanet on Mar 31, 1901<span class="citation">5</span> following the death of her husband on Feb 26, 1901; and on Elder Cottage, 23 Northdown Road, St Peters, Thanet on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">12</span>. <p>She died on Nov 18, 1915 on 23 Northdown Road, St Peters, Thanet, Kent<span class="citation">6</span> (senility, arterio-sclerosis, syncope) and was buried at St Peters Churchyard, St Peters on Nov 24, 1915.
