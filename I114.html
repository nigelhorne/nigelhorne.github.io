Mary Ann Mount (née Colyer) c1832 - 1920

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A charwoman and the child of George Colyer (an agricultural laborer) and Dorcas BelseyMary Colyer, the great-great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England c. 18321,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized in Blean, Kent, England on Jul 13, 1833 and married Abraham Mount (a mariner with whom she had 5 children: William, Elizabeth Ann, Edwin Henry, Mary A and Walter Charles, along with 1 surviving child) in Herne Bay, Kent, England on Oct 22, 18598 (Registry office).

Throughout her life, Mary lived in several places: on Salts Lane in Herne Bay on Apr 2, 18719; in Eddington, Kent, England on Apr 3, 188112; at 2 Salts Lane in Herne Bay on Apr 5, 18913; on 372 Merton Road, Wandsworth, London, England on Mar 31, 19014, when she was living with her son William, following the death of her husband on May 12, 1897; in hospital at Mitcham Workhouse, Holborn, London from Jun 16, 1903 to Jul 22, 1903; in hospital from Oct 11, 1904 to Dec 20, 1904; and in hospital on Tooting Bec Asylum, Church Lane, Tooting Common, London on Sep 7, 1906 and on Apr 2, 191110.

She died on Mar 21, 1920 at 164 St. John's Hill, Wandsworth, London, England7 (cardis Vascular degeneration).
