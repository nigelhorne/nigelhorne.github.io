The daughter of John Culmer (a parish clerk) and Sarah Hawkes, Frances Culmer, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England on Sep 19, 18071 and baptized there on Oct 11, 1807. She married George Roberts at St Mildred's Church, Preston, Kent, England on Jul 31, 1828.
