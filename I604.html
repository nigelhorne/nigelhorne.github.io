The child of John Culmer (a parish clerk) and Sarah Hawkes, Frances Culmer, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England on 19 Sept 18071 and baptised there on 11 Oct 1807. She married George Roberts at St Mildred's Church, Preston, Kent, England on 31 Jul 1828.

She died c. May 1844 in Eastry, Kent, England2.
