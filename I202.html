Ellen Mary Ann Payne (née Horne) 1872 - 1967

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The daughter of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper)Ellen Horne, the great-great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on Feb 14, 18721,2,3,4 and married Lendon Payne (a postman with whom she had 5 children: Bessie Charlotte, Thomas Tusting, Ellen Barbara, Lendon Harrison and Leslie Fitzhugh) at Warwick Gardens Methodist Chapel, Kensington, London, England on May 26, 18986.

Throughout her life, Ellen lived in several places: on Buddles Farm, Dane Court Road, St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 3, 18819; at 194 Reading Street, St Peters in Thanet on Apr 5, 18917; on 71 Grange Road in Margate on Apr 2, 19118; and in Wembley, London, England on Sep 29, 19392.

She died on Feb 15, 1967 in Brent, London, England5.
