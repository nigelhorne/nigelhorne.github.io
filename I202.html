Ellen Mary Ann Payne (née Horne) 1872 - 1967

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper), Ellen Horne, the great-great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on 14 Feb 18721,2,3,4 and married Lendon Payne (a postman with whom she had 5 children: Bessie Charlotte, Thomas Tusting, Ellen Barbara, Lendon Harrison and Leslie Fitzhugh) at Warwick Gardens Methodist Chapel, Kensington, London, England on 26 May 18986.

During her life, she was living at Buddles Farm, Dane Court Road, St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 3 Apr 18819; at 194 Reading Street, St Peters in Thanet on 5 Apr 18917; at 71 Grange Road in Margate on 2 Apr 19118 - less than a mile from her nephew Isaac Horne who was living at 4 Newington Terrace, St Lawrence in Thanet; and in Wembley, London, England on 29 Sept 19392.

She died on 15 Feb 1967 in Brent, London, England5.
