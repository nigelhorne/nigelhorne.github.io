John Bennett c1807 - 1895

An agricultural labourer and the child of William Bennet and Rebecca Trice, John Bennett, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Wickhambreaux, Kent, England c. 18071,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised there on 23 Aug 1807 and also married Mary Measday (with whom he had 9 children: Sarah Rebecca, Barbara, John, Mary, William, Charles, Samuel, George and Henry, along with 3 surviving children) there at St Andrew's Church on 14 May 18317 (Kent Family History Society CD22).

During his life, he was living in Preston, Kent, England in 18411; at Fourturnings, Preston, Wingham, Kent on 30 Mar 18518 and on 7 Apr 18619; in Preston in 18713; in Bramling, Littlebourne, Kent on 3 Apr 18814; and on Wingham Street in Wickhambreaux on 5 Apr 18915.

He died in Jul/Aug/Sep 1895 in Wingham, Kent, England6.
