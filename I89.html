John Bennett c1807 - 1895

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

An agricultural laborer and the child of William Bennet and Rebecca Trice, John Bennett, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Wickhambreaux, Kent, England c. 18071,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there on Aug 23, 1807 and also married Mary Measday (with whom he had 9 children: Sarah Rebecca, Barbara, John, Mary, William, Charles, Samuel, George and Henry, along with 3 surviving children) there at St Andrew's Church, on May 14, 18317 (Kent Family History Society CD22).

John spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Preston, Kent, England in 18411; at Fourturnings, Preston, Wingham on Mar 30, 18518 and on Apr 7, 18619; in Preston in 18713; in Bramling, Littlebourne on Apr 3, 18814; and on Wingham Street in Wickhambreaux on Apr 5, 18915.

He died in Jul/aug/sep 1895 in Wingham, Kent6.
