A general builder's laborer and the son of James Lawrence (an agricultural laborer) and Elizabeth Mount (a domestic servant)Edward Lawrence, the great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Sep 11, 18841,2,3,4,5, was baptized there on Nov 11, 1884 and married Mabel Waterman (with whom he had 1 child, Edward Douglas, ) in Thanet, Kent, England around Nov 19066.

Edward spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at 10 Claremont Gardens, Ramsgate on Apr 5, 18919 - less than a mile from his grandfather William Lawrence who was living at 3 Providence Cottage, St Lawrence in Thanet, when he was living with his brother Walter Charles and sister Emily Jane; at 2 Lyle Terrace, St Lawrence in Thanet on Mar 31, 190111; on 2 Clifton Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 191110 - less than a mile from his niece Lilian Florence, nephew Frederick James Cowell, nephew Charles John Cowell and sister Emily Jane Lawrence who were living on 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence in Thanet; and on 22 Grosvenor Road, Ramsgate in 19358, in 19367 and on Sep 29, 19393.
