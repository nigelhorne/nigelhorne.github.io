Charles Horn 1852 - 1929

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A waggoner on a farm and agricultural laborer and the son of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper)Charles Horn, the great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on Jul 28, 18521,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there on Oct 10, 1852 and married Jane Tutt (a laundress with whom he had 3 children: Charles Henry, Isaac and Edmund) at Mount Ephraim Baptist Chapel, Margate, Kent, England on Jun 9, 18838 (Mount Ephraim Chapel).

Charles spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: Northdown in Margate on Apr 2, 18711; at Drapers Cottage in Margate on Apr 5, 18919; at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence, Thanet on Mar 31, 190110, when he was living with his son Isaac, following the death of his wife on Jun 7, 1909; on Elder Cottage, 23 Northdown Road, St Peters, Thanet on Apr 2, 191111 (when he was living with his mother, Elizabeth Ann) following the death of his wife on Jun 7, 1909; and at Sacketts Hill Farm, St Peters, Thanet on Jun 19, 192112. Charles In 1921 he was working at George Phippott Farmer on Buddles Farm, Dane Court Road, Broadstairs, Kent, England.

He died on Jan 20, 1929 in Broadstairs, Kent, England7 (myocarditis and enlarged prostate) and was buried in St Peters after Jan 20, 1929.
