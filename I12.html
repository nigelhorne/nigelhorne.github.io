Charles Horn 1852 - 1929

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Census Index ] | [ Family History ]

A waggoner on a farm and agricultural laborer and the child of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper), Charles Horn, the great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on Jul 28, 18521,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there on Oct 10, 1852 and married Jane Tutt (a laundress with whom he had 3 children: Charles Henry, Isaac and Edmund) at Mount Ephraim Baptist Chapel, Margate, Kent, England on Jun 9, 18838 (Mount Ephraim Chapel).

During his life, he was living Northdown in Margate on Apr 2, 18711; at Drapers Cottage in Margate on Apr 5, 18919; at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent on Mar 31, 190110; and at Elder Cottage, 23 Northdown Road, St Peters, Thanet, Kent on Apr 2, 191111 (when he was living with his) following the death of his wife on Jun 7, 1909.

He died on Jan 20, 1929 in Broadstairs, Kent, England7 (myocarditis and enlarged prostate) and was buried in St Peters after Jan 20, 1929.
