Charles Horn 1852 - 1929

A waggoner on a farm and agricultural labourer and the son of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper)Charles Horn, the great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 28 Jul 18521,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised there on 10 Oct 1852 and married Jane Tutt (a laundress with whom he had 3 children: Charles Henry, Isaac and Edmund) at Mount Ephraim Baptist Chapel, Margate, Kent, England on 9 Jun 18838 (Mount Ephraim Chapel).

Charles spent all of his life in Kent in England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: Northdown in Margate on 2 Apr 18711; at Drapers Cottage in Margate on 5 Apr 18919; at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence, Thanet on 31 Mar 190110, when he was living with his son Isaac, following the death of his wife on 7 Jun 1909; on Elder Cottage, 23 Northdown Road, St Peters, Thanet on 2 Apr 191111 (when he was living with his mother, Elizabeth Ann) following the death of his wife on 7 Jun 1909; and at Sacketts Hill Farm, St Peters, Thanet on 19 Jun 192112. Charles In 1921 he was working at George Phippott Farmer on Buddles Farm, Dane Court Road, Broadstairs, Kent, England.

He died on 20 Jan 1929 in Broadstairs, Kent, England7 (myocarditis and enlarged prostate) and was buried in St Peters after 20 Jan 1929.
