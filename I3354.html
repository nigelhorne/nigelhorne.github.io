A grocer and confectioner and the child of James Port (a barber) and Jane Studwick (a laundress), Charles Port, the second cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England c. Feb 18541,2, was baptised there at St Martin on 12 Nov 1854 and married Martha Jordan (a shop keeper with whom he had 8 children: Edith M, Martha E, Harry, Nellie, James Albert, Charles M, George Arthur and William C, along with 1 surviving child) at St Alphege, Canterbury, Kent, England on 17 Jan 18754.
