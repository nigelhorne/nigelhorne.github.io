The 5th of 8 children of Thomas Sergeant and Telitha Crance, Isaac Sargent, the second cousin four-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Allen, Indiana, USA on 29 Oct 18601,2 and, and was orphaned at the age of 15 following the death of his father in 1873 and mother in 1875.
