A cutter's pupil and textile warehouse salesman and the child of Benjamin Cowell (a woollen warehouse foreman) and Agnes Jacobs, Stanley Cowell, the third cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Chatham, Kent, England on 8 Jan 18931,2,3 and. He was married twice - to Mercia Bishop (on 6 Jun 1925 in Medway, Kent, England) Beryl Mold (c. Aug 1963 in Croydon, Surrey, England, following the death of Mercia Florence Isabel on 11 Jul 1961)6. He has 1 surviving child with Mercia Bishop, Judith F M.

During his life, he was living at 5 Hope Street in Chatham on 31 Mar 19011; at 157 Glencoe Road in Chatham on 2 Apr 19118; at 74 Burnt Ash Road, Lewisham, London, England in 19297; at 37 Felhampton Road, Woolwich, London on 29 Sept 19392; and at Elliott House, 22 Reculver Road, Beltinge, Kent in 1983.

He died on 1 Jan 1984 in Canterbury, Kent, England4,5.
