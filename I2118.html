The child of John Culmer (an agricultural laborer) and Phoebe Port, Harriet Culmer, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Hoath, Kent, England c. 18301,2, was baptized there on Oct 31, 1830 and married Benjamin Boodle (an agricultural laborer with whom she had 12 children: Francis, George, John, Ann, Edward, Henry, Mary, William, Jane, Esther, Thomas and Alfred, along with 1 surviving child) at Ss Cosmus & Damian's Church, Blean, Kent, England on Oct 29, 18484.

During her life, she was living at Clapham Hill in Blean on Mar 30, 18511; at New Road in Blean on Apr 7, 18615; on Fordwich Lane, Canterbury, Kent on Apr 2, 18716; in Blean on Apr 3, 18817; and at Plough Row, Harbledown, Kent on Mar 31, 19012.

She died c. May 1904 in Bridge, Kent, England3.
