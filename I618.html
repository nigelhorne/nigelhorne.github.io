Mary Chandler (née Culmer) c1720 - 1760

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Daniel Culmer and Jane Neame, Mary Culmer, the seven times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Preston, Kent, England c. 17201 and baptized there on Oct 27, 1720. She married Thomas Chandler at St Nicholas Church, Ash, Kent, England on Oct 21, 17451, which is also where she died in 1760.
