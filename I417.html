Jane Foord, the wife of James Tutt (the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England in Jul 18541,2,3,4,5,6,7 and married James (a general laborer with whom she had 8 children: Jane Eliza Caroline, James Richard Edmund, Jessie Maria, Edmund Thomas, Lily, Harriet, Rose Elizabeth and Frank, along with 1 surviving child) at St Lawrence Parish Church, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 29, 18769 (KFHS CD 29).
