The child of Edward Dale and Ann Stone, Edward Dale, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Nackington, Kent, England in 18061,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9, was baptised in Canterbury, Kent, England on 16 Nov 1806 and also married Rebecca Crockford (with whom he had 8 children: John, George, Mary, Alfred, Eliza, Edward, Sophia and Sarah Ann) there at St Mary Northgate on 17 Jun 182510. Like his father, he was an agricultural labourer.

During his life, he was living in Chartham Hatch, Kent, England on 6 Jun 184117 and on 30 Mar 185118 (the same place as his father had been living on 6 Jun 1841); at Mount Pleasant, Chartham, Kent on 7 Apr 186116; at The Green, Chartham, Kent on 2 Apr 187115 and on 3 Apr 188114; and at Milton Cottage, Chartham, Kent on 5 Apr 189119, when he was living with his daughter Sophia, following the death of his wife on 21 Jun 1888.

He died on 5 Mar 1894 in Thanington, Kent, England8 (senile decay and bronchitis) and was buried at St Mary's Church, Chartham, Kent on 8 Mar 189411,12,13.
