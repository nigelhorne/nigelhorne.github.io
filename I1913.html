Albert Thomas Cowell cMay 1894 - 1916

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A colliery cabin man and the child of Albert Cowell (an agricultural labourer) and Ellen YoungAlbert Cowell, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Dambridge, Kent, England c. May 18941.

Throughout his life, he lived at Adisham Street, Adisham, Kent, England on 31 Mar 19014; and at Church Street, Nonington, Kent on 2 Apr 19115. He served in the Army from 26 Aug 1914 to 19 Mar 1916 (regiment: Buffs (East Kent Regiment); Service number: GS/EK/319).

He died on 19 Mar 1916 at Bethune Hospital, Bethune, Hauts-de-France, France2,3 (died of wounds) and was buried at Bethune Town Cemetery, Bethune on 20 Mar 1916.
