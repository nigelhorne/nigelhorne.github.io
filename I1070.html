Martha Horn (née Wellard) cNov 1839 - 1936

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Martha Wellard, the wife of George Horn (the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England c. Nov 18391,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there on Nov 24, 1839 and also married George (a brewery laborer with whom she had 6 children: George, Mary Jane, Henry William, Fanny, Ellen Martha and Florence, along with 1 surviving child) there, on Nov 5, 18637 (Oct/Nov/Dec).

Martha spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at 76 Queen Street, Ramsgate, Kent, England on Apr 2, 187111, when she was living with her son George and daughter Mary Jane; at Queen Street in Ramsgate on Apr 3, 18819; in Ramsgate in 18913 and in 19014; at Paradise in Ramsgate on Mar 31, 19018 - less than a mile from her daughter Florence Horne who was living at 8 Paradise in Ramsgate; and in Ramsgate on Apr 2, 19115.

She died in 1936 in Thanet, Kent, England.
