Martha Wellard, the wife of George Horn (the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England c. Nov 18391,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised there on 24 Nov 1839 and also married George (a brewery labourer with whom she had 6 children: George, Mary Jane, Henry William, Fanny, Ellen Martha and Florence, along with 1 surviving child) there on 5 Nov 18637 (Oct/Nov/Dec).

During her life, she was living at 76 Queen Street, Ramsgate, Kent, England on 2 Apr 187111; at Queen Street in Ramsgate on 3 Apr 18819; in Ramsgate in 18913 and in 19014; at Paradise in Ramsgate on 31 Mar 19018; and in Ramsgate on 2 Apr 19115.

She died in 1936 in Thanet, Kent, England.
