The child of James Page and Elizabeth Sellis, Stanley Page, the third cousin once-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England on 13 May 18871, was baptised there at St John The Baptist on 28 Aug 1887. He married Beatrice Smith (with whom he had 1 surviving child, ) in Elham, Kent, England around Nov 19105. In 1917 he was serving in the military in Iraq (soldier Number: 121686, Rank: Gunner, Corps: Royal Garrison Artillery).

He died on 24 Jul 1917 in Baghdad, Iraq2,3,4 (heat Stroke) and was buried there at Baghdad War Cemetery (North Gate) after 24 Jul 19176.
