Feb 18591,2,3,4,5, was baptized there on Apr 24, 1859 and also married William Hobday (an ironmonger's assistant with whom she had 2 children: Ethel Elizabeth and Florence Evelyn, along with 1 surviving child) there, on Mar 19, 1882.
