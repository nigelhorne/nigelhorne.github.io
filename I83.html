Charlotte Culmer (née Coleman) c1829 - 1903

A pauper and the child of Henry Coleman (a gardener and domestic) and Elizabeth Adams, Charlotte Coleman, the great-great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Sarre, Kent, England c. 18291,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptised there on 22 May 1829 and married Charles Culmer (a farm waggoner with whom she had 9 children: Ann, Henry, Charles, Elizabeth, Emma, John, George, Jane and Frances, along with 5 surviving children) in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on 21 May 18509 (St. Nicholas At Wade, Kent, England).

During her life, she was living at her birthplace on 6 Jun 184114; at Row, Monkton, Kent on 30 Mar 185113; on Monkton Street, Monkton, Kent on 7 Apr 18613; at The Street in St Nicholas-at-Wade on 2 Apr 187110 and on 3 Apr 18815; and at Street in Sarre on 5 Apr 189111 following the death of her husband on 17 Aug 1881 and on 31 Mar 190112.

She died on 23 Sept 1903 in Sarre8 (shock from burns).
