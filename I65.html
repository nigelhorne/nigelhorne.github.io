The child of James Spratt and Sarah Spain, James Spratt, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England c. Feb 18501,2,3,4 and baptised there on 17 Feb 1850. He married Emma Thunder (a publican) at St Martin, Kentish Town, London, England on 1 Jan 18856. Like his father, he was an agricultural labourer.

During his life, he was living at Crump's Farm in St Nicholas-at-Wade on 30 Mar 18511; at his birthplace in 18612; at Rising Sun, 103 Reculver Road, Beltinge, Kent, England on 5 Apr 18918, on 31 Mar 19019, in 1902 and in 1903; and at Sidney Villa, Reculver Road in Beltinge in 1909.

He died on 26 Apr 1909 in Blean, Kent, England5.
