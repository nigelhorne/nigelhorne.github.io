Jane Horn (née Tutt) 1849 - 1909

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A laundress and the child of Edmund Tutt (a sawyer) and Caroline Page (a laundress), Jane Tutt, the great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Buckland, Kent, England on 12 May 18491,2,3,4,5,6,7,8 and married Charles Horn (a waggoner on a farm with whom she had 3 children: Charles Henry, Isaac and Edmund) at Mount Ephraim Baptist Chapel, Margate, Kent, England on 9 Jun 18839 (Mount Ephraim Chapel).

During her life, she was living at 40 George Street in Buckland on 30 Mar 185114; at 13 Shooter's Hill in Buckland on 7 Apr 186112; at 7 Market Place, Dover, Kent on 2 Apr 18713; at 18 Clarence Terrace, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent on 3 Apr 188113 (when she was living with her); at Drapers Cottage in Margate on 5 Apr 18915; and at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent on 31 Mar 19016 - less than a mile from her nephew Isaac Horne who was living at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent.

She died on 7 Jun 1909 at 4 Convent Cottages, Reading Street, St Peters, Thanet, Kent8 (chronic heart disease) and was buried there on 11 Jun 1909.
