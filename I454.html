Edith Kate Simmons (née Dale) 1903 - 1989

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A cashier and the child of Leonard Dale (a boiler attendant brickworks) and Ellen Smith, Edith Dale, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Temple Ewell, Kent, England on 16 Jun 19031,2,3,4,5,6 and baptised there at Ss Peter & Paul on 26 Jul 1903. She married Arthur Simmons (a plasterer) in The Register Office, Crowborough, Sussex, England on 2 Nov 19317.

During her life, she was living at 12 Douglas Road, Dover, Kent, England on 2 Apr 19118; at 52 Widred Road in Dover on 19 Jun 19212; and in Cuckfield, Sussex, England on 29 Sept 19393. In 1921 she was working at Timothy White Company in Dover.

She died on 11 Dec 1989 at The Green, Newport Pagnell, Buckinghamshire, England5.
