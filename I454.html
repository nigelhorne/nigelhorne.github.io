Edith Kate Simmons (née Dale) 1903 - 1989

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A cashier and the daughter of Leonard Dale (a boiler attendant brickworks) and Ellen Smith, Edith Dale, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Temple Ewell, Kent, England on Jun 16, 19031,2,3,4,5,6 and baptized there at Ss Peter & Paul on Jul 26, 1903. She married Arthur Simmons (a plasterer) in The Register Office, Crowborough, Sussex, England on Nov 2, 19317.

Throughout her life, Edith lived in several places: on 12 Douglas Road, Dover, Kent, England on Apr 2, 19118; on 52 Widred Road in Dover on Jun 19, 19212; and in Cuckfield, Sussex on Sep 29, 19393. Edith In 1921 she was working at Timothy White Company in Dover, Kent, England.

She died on Dec 11, 1989 at The Green, Newport Pagnell, Buckinghamshire, England5.
