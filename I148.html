Johanna Foreman (née Brooker) 1811 - 1885

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A pauper char woman and the child of Thomas Brooker and Sarah Crow, Johanna Brooker, the 3 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on 13 Dec 18111,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised there at St Mary the Virgin Church on 5 Jan 1812 was married twice - to Daniel Edwards (on 18 Sept 1829 in Minster) Thomas Foreman (on 22 Jun 1856 in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England, following the death of Daniel in Jul/Aug/Sep 1847)8. She had 2 children, both with Daniel Edwards: Johanna Brooker and Stephen.

During her life, she was living in St Lawrence on 6 Apr 18411; at Chapel Row, St Lawrence in Thanet on 30 Mar 185111; and in St Lawrence in 18613, in 18714 and in 18815.

She died on 4 Apr 1885 at 3 Claremont Gardens, St Lawrence in Thanet6,7 (heart failure and bronchitis. Death certificate says aged 64, but I believe that is a mistake, it should read 74.) and was buried there on 8 Apr 18857,9,10.
