A fitter and waterworks main layer heavy and the son of James Tutt (a general laborer) and Jane Foord (a general shop)James Tutt, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England on Dec 7, 18771,2,3,4, was baptized there at St Lukes on Aug 13, 1878 and also married Clara Ansley (with whom he had 1 child, James Alfred William) there at Holy Trinity Church, on Apr 24, 19055.

James spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at 18 Clarence Terrace in Ramsgate on Apr 3, 18816; on Hillbrow Road, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Mar 31, 19011 - less than a mile from his cousin on his father's side Charles Henry Horne, cousin on his father's side Edmund Horne and aunt Jane Tutt who were living at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence in Thanet and his father James who was living on 8 Hillbrow Road, St Lawrence in Thanet -; at 5 Pleasant Place, Margate on Apr 2, 19112; and at 6 Pleasant Place, Margate on Sep 29, 19393.
