Stephen Edwards 1847 - 1923

A stationery engine driver and the child of Daniel Edwards (a laborer) and Johanna Brooker (a pauper char woman), Stephen Edwards, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England in Apr/may/jun 18471,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptized there on Jun 6, 1847 and also married Ann Lasslett (with whom he had 7 children: Harry Walter, Edward Frederick, Florence Maria, Alfred W, Helen Gertrude, Sydney Horace and Percy Leopold, along with 3 surviving children) there on Mar 26, 18709.

During his life, he was living at Chapel Row, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Mar 30, 185115; in Vessels, Misc Ships at Sea or Abroad, England in 18612; at Chapel Row on Apr 2, 187113; at his birthplace in 18814; at Whitehall Waterworks, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 3, 188114; West Dumpton in Thanet on Apr 5, 189112; at Waterworks Cottage, St Lawrence in Thanet on Mar 31, 190111; at Southwood Pumping Station, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 191110; and at Waterworks House, Southwood Road, St Lawrence in Thanet in 1923.

He died on Apr 28, 1923 at Waterworks House, Southwood Road, Ramsgate, Kent.
