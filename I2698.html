The 3rd of 5 children of George Austin (a baker's assistant) and Susannah Dale, Avis Austin, (also known as Diana Austin) the third cousin once-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Chartham, Kent, England on 28 Oct 19081,2, was baptised there on 29 Nov 1908 was married 3 times - to Khim Kok (c. Aug 1936 in Kensington, London, England) Dennis Willcox Wan Liem3. She had 6 children: Dianne and Gillian Cara with unknown father, and 4 surviving children.

During her life, she was living at 2 Heaton Cottages, Heaton Road, Canterbury, Kent, England on 2 Apr 19111; and at 43 Tetherdown, Hornsey, London in 19294. In 1929 she travelled from Plymouth, Devon, England (aboard the ship Rawalpindi) to Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, departing on 10 May, arriving later that same year.

She died on 22 Jan 1994 in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.
