Bernard Walter Standing 1925 - 1970

A typewriter mechanic and workshop manager and the son of William Standing (a laundryman) and Eliza Parr, Bernard Standing, the fourth cousin once-removed on the father's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Ilford, London, England on Sep 7, 1925<span class="citation">1,2,3,4</span> and. He married Rose Jackson (a shorthand typist with whom he had 1 surviving child) in Ilford on Jun 4, 1949<span class="citation">6</span>.</p><p>Throughout his life, he lived at his birthplace in 1949 (the same place as his father had been living on Jun 19, 1921); and at 71 Berkeley Avenue in Ilford in 1964<span class="citation">7</span> and in 1970. <p>He died on Oct 21, 1970 at King George Hospital in Ilford<span class="citation">2,4,5</span> (cardiac infraction and coronary thrombosis) and was buried at Barkingside Garden of Rest Cemetery, Barkingside, London, England after Oct 21, 1970.
