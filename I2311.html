The child of Joseph Dane (a master mariner, licensed victualler) and Emma Stupple (a domestic general servant), Daisy Dane, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Faversham, Kent, England on 30 Sept 19161 and married Bernard Mullen (with whom she had 2 children: Bernice and Anthony B, along with 1 surviving child) at Faversham Parish Church in Faversham on 16 Apr 19382. On 29 Sept 1939, she was living at 6 The Hornet, Chichester, Sussex, England.

She died in Aug 2003 in Caboolture, Queensland, Australia.
