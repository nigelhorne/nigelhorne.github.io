Charles Culmer 1852 - 1915

An agricultural labourer and the child of Charles Culmer (a farm waggoner) and Charlotte Coleman (a pauper), Charles Culmer, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Monkton, Kent, England on 5 Dec 18521,2,3,4,5,6,7 and married Emma Beak (with whom he had 11 children: Henry Gage, Charlotte Ann, Alfred, Charles, William James, George, Fred, Edith Emily, Edward, Arthur Thomas and Georgina Hannah, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mildred's Church, Preston, Kent, England on 22 Aug 18748 (Jul/Aug/Sep).

During his life, he was living at Monkton Street in Monkton on 7 Apr 18611; on Monkton Parsonage, Monkton Street in Monkton on 2 Apr 187113; at The Square, Stourmouth, Kent on 3 Apr 188112; in Westmarsh, Kent, England on 5 Apr 189110; at 1 Chalk Hole Cottages, Flete, Kent on 31 Mar 19019; and at Newport Cottage, Lydden Farm, Garlinge, Kent on 2 Apr 19115.

He died on 21 Jun 1915 in Thanet, Kent, England7.
