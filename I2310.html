The daughter of Joseph Dane (a master mariner and licensed victualler) and Emma Stupple (a domestic general servant)Daisy Dane, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Faversham, Kent, England on Sep 30, 19161 and married Bernard Mullen (with whom she had 2 children: Bernice and Anthony B, along with 1 surviving child) at Faversham Parish Church in Faversham on Apr 16, 19382.
