The child of Thomas Poysden and SarahAnna Poysden, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England c. 18051,2, was baptized in Canterbury, Kent, England on May 1, 1808 and married William Strand (an agricultural laborer with whom she had 4 children: Eliza, William, Joseph Thomas and Edwin, along with 2 surviving children) in Sturry, Kent, England on Jun 1, 18337.

Throughout her life, she lived in Chislet, Kent, England in 18411; and in Upstreet, Kent, England on Mar 30, 18519.
