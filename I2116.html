The child of John Culmer (an agricultural laborer) and Phoebe PortHarriet Culmer, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Hoath, Kent, England c. 18301,2, was baptized there on Oct 31, 1830 and married Benjamin Boodle (an agricultural laborer with whom she had 12 children: Francis, George, John, Ann, Edward, Henry, Mary, William, Jane, Esther, Thomas and Alfred, along with 1 surviving child) at Ss Cosmus & Damian's Church, Blean, Kent, England on Oct 29, 18484.

Harriet spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Clapham Hill in Blean on Mar 30, 18511; at New Road in Blean on Apr 7, 18617 (when she was living with her parents); on Fordwich Lane, Canterbury on Apr 2, 18716; in Blean on Apr 3, 18815 (the same place as her parents had been living on Jun 6, 1841); and at Plough Row, Harbledown on Mar 31, 19012.

She died c. May 1904 in Bridge, Kent, England3.
