The child of James Lawrence (an agricultural labourer) and Elizabeth Mount (a domestic servant), Elizabeth Lawrence, the great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 28 May 18911,2,3,4,5, was baptised there on 28 Jun 1891 and married Harry Hodgman (a farm houseman with whom she had 3 children: Elizabeth A S, Harry J and Kathleen C) in Thanet, Kent, England around Feb 19158.

During her life, she was living in Staple, Kent, England on 31 Mar 190110; at Oast Cottages, Cleve, Monkton, Kent on 2 Apr 19119 - less than a mile from her half-sister Ethel May who was living at 2 Oast Cottage, Cleve, Monkton, Kent and her half-sister Florence Eva who was living at 2 Oast Cottages, Cleve, Monkton, Kent -; at Hoaden Cottages, Ash, Kent on 29 Sept 19392; and at 89 The Street, Ash, Kent in 1969.

She died on 3 May 1969 in Canterbury, Kent, England4,6,7 and was buried at Saint Nicholas Churchyard, Ash, Kent after 3 May 1969.
