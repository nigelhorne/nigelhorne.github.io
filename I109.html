Elizabeth Annie Hodgman (née Lawrence) 1891 - 1969

The daughter of James Lawrence (an agricultural labourer) and Elizabeth Mount (a domestic servant)Elizabeth Lawrence, the great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 28 May 18911,2,3,4,5, was baptised there on 28 Jun 1891 and married Harry Hodgman (a farm houseman with whom she had 3 children: Elizabeth A S, Harry J and Kathleen C) in Thanet, Kent, England around Feb 19158.

Elizabeth spent all of her life in Kent in England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: in Staple, Kent, England on 31 Mar 190110; at Oast Cottages, Cleve, Monkton on 2 Apr 19119; at Hoaden Cottages, Ash on 29 Sept 19392; and at 89 The Street, Ash in 1969.

She died on 3 May 1969 in Canterbury, Kent, England4,6,7 and was buried at Saint Nicholas Churchyard, Ash, Kent after 3 May 1969.
