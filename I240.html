Eliza Tunbridge (née Tutt) 1847 - c1930

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A general servant and the daughter of Edmund Tutt (a sawyer) and Caroline Page (a laundress)Eliza Tutt, the great-great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Buckland, Kent, England on 15 Sept 18471,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptised there at St Andrew's Church on 16 Apr 1848 and married Thomas Tunbridge (a dairyman with whom she had 5 children: Harry, Lily, Frank, Walter and Albert) in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 22 Aug 187410 (Jul/Aug/Sep).

Eliza spent all of her life in Kent in England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at 40 George Street in Buckland on 30 Mar 185115, when she was living with her sister, Jane; at Shooter's Hill in Buckland on 7 Apr 186114; in Dover, Kent, England in 18713; in Hougham, Kent, England in 18814; at 9 Elms Cottage in Hougham on 5 Apr 189112; and on 5 Eaton Road in Dover on 31 Mar 190111 and on 2 Apr 191113.

She died c. May 1930 in Dover9.
