An ironmonger's shop's assistant and the son of James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company) and Emily LawrenceJames Cowell, the uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Sutton, Kent, England on Jul 30, 19161,2,3,4,5 and was married twice - to Lydia Bonner (on Sep 10, 1938 in Margate, Kent, England) Lily Mitchell (c. Feb 1954 in Thanet, Kent, England)6. He had 4 children: Ronald J and Wendy J with Lydia Florence, and 2 surviving children.

James spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster, Thanet on Jun 19, 19219, when he was living with his sister, Freda Violet and brothers, Charles John, Henry Richard Stephen and Frederick James; on 3 Church Road in Margate on Sep 29, 19391 - less than a mile from his brother Henry Richard Stephen who was living at 22 Church Square in Margate; at 15 Salmestone Rise in Margate in 19538; at Salmestone Rise in Margate in 1954; and at 18 Perkins Avenue in Margate in 19557 which is where he died on Mar 10, 19874 (coronary artery thrombosis and atheroma).
