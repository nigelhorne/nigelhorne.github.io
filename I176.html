A bricklayer's labourer and the child of Isaac Stuple and Ann Mount, Thomas Stupple, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne Bay, Kent, England c. 18311,2,3, was baptised in Herne, Kent, England on 12 Jun 1831 and married Ann Gammon (a charwoman with whom he had 7 children: Caroline, Jesse G, Elizabeth, Nellie, Emma Jane, James S and Edward) at Christ Church in Herne Bay on 5 Jun 18655.

During his life, he was living in Vessels, Royal Navy, England in 18611; and at Nursery Gardens in Herne in 18812 and on 3 Apr 18816.

He died c. Nov 1899 in Blean, Kent, England3,4.
