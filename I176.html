A bricklayer's laborer and the child of Isaac Stuple and Ann Mount, Thomas Stupple, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne Bay, Kent, England c. 18311,2,3, was baptized in Herne, Kent, England on Jun 12, 1831 and married Ann Gammon (a charwoman with whom he had 7 children: Caroline, Jesse G, Elizabeth, Nellie, Emma Jane, James S and Edward) at Christ Church in Herne Bay on Jun 5, 18655.

During his life, he was living in Vessels, Royal Navy, England in 18611; and at Nursery Gardens in Herne in 18812 and on Apr 3, 18816.
