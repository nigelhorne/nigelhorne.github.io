The twin brother of Cecil John and the 3rd of 4 children of Bertie Barton (a builder & public work's contractor) and Lillian Spratt (a domestic general servant), Cyril Barton, the first cousin once-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Blean, Kent, England on 6 Sept 19141,2,3,4,5 along with his twin brother Cecil John. He married Joyce Thomas at Cavendish Baptist Church, Cavendish Street, Ramsgate, Kent, England on 26 Jun 19377.

During his life, he was living at 36 Grange Road in Ramsgate on 29 Sept 19391; at 38 Grange Road in Ramsgate in 195310; and at 36 Grange Road in Ramsgate in 1955, in 1957, in 19639, in 1965 and in 19748.

He died in Jun 1984 in Thanet, Kent, England4,6.
