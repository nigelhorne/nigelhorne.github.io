James Frederick Cowell 1887 - 1974

A seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company and the son of John Cowell (a farm waggoner) and Emma CulmerJames Cowell, (also known as Freddie Cowell) the grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Upstreet, Kent, England on Mar 2, 18871,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptized in Chislet, Kent, England on May 22, 1887 went to Westbrook Sunday School, Westbrook, Thanet, Kent, England He married Emily Lawrence (with whom he had 9 children: Frederick James, Charles John, Emily Edith, Henry Richard Stephen, Reginald Albert, James Sidney, Dorothy Helen, Freda Violet and Joyce Margaret) at Thanet Registry Office in Thanet on Jan 7, 19099 (Married at Thanet Registry Office).

James spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at his birthplace on Apr 5, 18911; on 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 191111; at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Jun 19, 192112, in 1935, in 1936 and on Sep 29, 19393; and at 37 Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet in 195310. James On Jun 19, 1921 he was working at Mr Burgess Works Dept Air Ministry at Manston Aerodrome, Minster, Thanet, Kent, England.

He died on May 27, 1974 at 106 Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet5,8 and was buried at Minster Cemetery, Tothill Street, Minster on May 31, 1974 (a drizzly day) following his funeral on Thanet Crematorium, Manston Road, Margate, Kent, England8.
