Isaac Horne 1867 - 1948

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A gardener domestic servant and the son of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper), Isaac Horne, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on Jun 18, 18671,2,3,4,5,6,7,8 and married Lucy Stockbridge (a dressmaker with whom he had 2 children: Edward John and Norman George) at Cavendish Baptist Church, Cavendish Street, Ramsgate, Kent, England on Jun 1, 19079.

Isaac spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at Northdown, Margate on Apr 2, 187110; on Buddles Farm, Dane Court Road, St Peters, Thanet on Apr 3, 188111; at 194 Reading Street, St Peters, Thanet on Apr 5, 189112, when he was living with his sister, Ellen Mary Ann; at 1 Welbeck Terrace, Reading Street, St Peters, Thanet on Mar 31, 19014 (the same place as his father had been living in 1895); at 23 Victoria Avenue, Northdown, Margate on Apr 2, 191113; and on The Cottage, Fairfield Road, St Peters, Thanet on Jun 19, 192114, on Sep 29, 19396 and in 1948 which is where he died in Sep 19487.
