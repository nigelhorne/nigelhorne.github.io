Ernest Benjamin Cowell 1883 - 1953

A mechanical engineering draughtsman and insurance agent and the child of Benjamin Cowell (a woollen warehouse foreman) and Agnes Jacobs, Ernest Cowell, the third cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Chatham, Kent, England on 9 Jul 18831,2,3,4,5 and was married twice - to Ethel Steddy (in 1907 in Medway, Kent, England) Mabel Heath (c. May 1939 in Strood, Kent, England, following the death of Ethel Maud c. Nov 1937)7. He had 1 child with Ethel Steddy, Ernest Frederick William.

During his life, he was living at 5 Hope Street in Chatham on 5 Apr 189115 and on 31 Mar 190114; at 1 Campbell Road, Gravesend, Kent on 2 Apr 19111; and at 205 Old Road West, Gravesend, Kent in 193813, on 29 Sept 193916 and in 195312.

He died on 6 Sept 1953 at Joyce Green Hospital, Dartford, Kent3,6 and was buried at Municipal Cemetery, Gravesend, Kent on 11 Sept 1953 (an overcast day)8,9,10,11.
