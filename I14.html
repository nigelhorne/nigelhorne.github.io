The 6th of 7 children of Isaac Horne (a hay trusser & thatcher heavy worker market gardener) and May Spratt, Frederick Horne, the uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on Nov 23, 19201,2,3,4. He married Ivy Dixon in Thanet, Kent, England around Aug 19615.
