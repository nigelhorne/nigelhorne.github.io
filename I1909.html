May 18541, was baptized there on Jul 23, 1854 and married Ellen Young (with whom he had 5 children: Jim, Florrie Hannah, Nellie Sarah, Albert Thomas and Annie Emily, along with 2 surviving children) in Faversham, Kent, England on Nov 6, 18863.
