Bertie John Barton 1883 - 1960

A builder & public work's contractor, Bertie Barton, the husband of Lillian Spratt (the great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Reculver, Kent, England on Oct 21, 18831,2,3,4,5, was baptized there at St Mary on Dec 9, 1883 and married Lillian (a domestic general servant with whom he had 4 children: Hilda May, Arthur George, Cyril Henry and Cecil John) in Thanet, Kent, England on Dec 23, 19057.

During his life, he was living in Hastings, Sussex, England in Apr 18995; at Phyllis Bungalow, Osborne Gardens in Reculver on Apr 2, 19118; and at Three Ways, Reculver Road, Herne Bay, Kent on Sep 29, 19392. During 1919, he was serving in the military (army labor corps - private).

He died on Nov 17, 1960 in Bridge, Kent, England6.
