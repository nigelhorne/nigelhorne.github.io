Bertie John Barton 1883 - 1960

Bertie Barton, the husband of Lillian Spratt (the great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Reculver, Kent, England on 21 Oct 18831,2,3,4,5, was baptised there at St Mary on 9 Dec 1883 and married Lillian (a domestic general servant with whom he had 4 children: Hilda May, Arthur George, Cyril Henry and Cecil John) in Thanet, Kent, England on 23 Dec 19057.

Throughout his life, Bertie lived in several places: in Hastings, Sussex, England in Apr 18995; at Phyllis Bungalow, Osborne Gardens in Reculver on 2 Apr 19118; and on Three Ways, Reculver Road, Herne Bay, Kent on 29 Sept 19392. During 1919, he was serving in the Military (army labour corps - private).

He died on 17 Nov 1960 in Bridge, Kent, England6.
