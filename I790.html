Henry Hogbin 1825 - 1878

Henry Hogbin, the husband of Jane Culmer (the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Preston, Kent, England in 18251,2,3,4,5. He married Jane in Eastry, Kent, England in 1849.

Henry spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in Cheriton, Kent, England in 18411; and in Stourmouth, Kent, England in 18512, in 18613 and in 18714. That is also where he died in Jul/aug/sep 1878 in Eastry5.
