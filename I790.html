Henry Hogbin, the husband of Jane Culmer (the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Preston, Kent, England in 1825<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5</span>. He married Jane in Eastry, Kent, England in 1849.</p><p>Henry spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in Cheriton, Kent in 1841<span class="citation">1</span>; and in Stourmouth, Kent in 1851<span class="citation">2</span>, in 1861<span class="citation">3</span> and in 1871<span class="citation">4</span>. That is also where he died in Jul/aug/sep 1878 in Eastry<span class="citation">5</span>.
