Annie Eldridge, the wife of Albert Edward Cowell (the first cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne), was born in Nackington, Kent, England on Oct 1, 18971,2. She married Albert (a farm worker horseman) in Thanet, Kent, England around Nov 19355 (Oct-Nov-Dec).

Throughout her life, Annie lived in several places: on Nackington Road in Nackington on Mar 31, 19016; at Church Cottages in Nackington on Apr 2, 19117; and at 9 High Street, Manston, Kent on Sep 29, 19391. She served in the Army from Jan 2, 1918 to Mar 5, 1919 (queen Mary's Army Auxiliary Corps).

She died c. Feb 1947 in Thanet3,4.
