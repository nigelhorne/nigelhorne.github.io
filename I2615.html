A legal secretary and the child of Levi Carley and Rosamund Flaxman, Hilda Carley, the third cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Keppel, Saskatchewan, Canada on 27 Nov 1916. She went to Technical Collegiate, Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada

During her life, she was living in Perdue, Saskatchewan in 1921; and at 430 Avenue J North, Saskatchewan in 1949.

She died on 17 Aug 2015 in Stensrud Lodge, Saskatchewan (hilda Dorothy Carley; 1916 - 2015; We regret to announce the passing of Hilda Dorothy Carley on Monday, August 17, 2015 at Stensrud Lodge. Hilda was born on November 27, 1916 on the homestead at Keppel, SK. She took her schooling in Saskatoon at Westmount School and at the Technical Collegiate. Hilda's working life was as a legal secretary, having worked in Winnipeg, Kelowna and Vancouver, but she always came back to Saskatoon. Hilda was predeceased by Mother and Father, Rosamond (Flaxman) Carley and Levi JohnCarley. Also by sisters Daphne, Gwen, Phyllis, and brothers, Leslie, Jack (WWII RCAF, 1942) and Gordon. She is survived by Muriel Hooper (Jack), Harold of Calgary and Richard of Sausalito, California, and by many nieces; nephews and cousins. Hilda was a Brownie and Guide leader and excelled at the piano having obtained her grade 10 at RCM. Cremation has taken place and interment will be held at a later date at Woodlawn Cemetery. We would like to thank the caring staff at Stensrud Lodge, especially nurse Kelene. Condolences may be sent to mail@saskatoonfuneralhome.com. Arrangements have been entrusted to SASKATOON FUNERAL HOME (244-5577)).
