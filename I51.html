Harry Dale Spratt 1890 - 1953

A carter and motor driver and the son of John Spratt (an agricultural laborer) and Sophia DaleHarry Spratt, the great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Jun 21, 18901,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9, was baptized there on Jul 20, 1890 and married Edith Anstey (with whom he had 1 child, Violet Lilian, ) at All Saints Church, Westbrook, Kent, England on Nov 22, 191310.

Throughout his life, Harry lived in several places: at Shuart Cottage, Podden Street in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 5, 189113; at Cottage, Nash Court, Garlinge, Kent on Mar 31, 190111 and on Apr 2, 191112; and on 1 Hill View Cottages, Twenties, Shottendane Road, Margate, Kent in 19156, in 1923, in 1929 and on Sep 29, 19394 which is where he died on Oct 7, 19537. On Dec 10, 1915 he was serving in the Army.
