A boatman and the child of Abraham Mount (a mariner) and Mary Colyer (a charwoman), Edwin Mount, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England on 4 Aug 18691,2,3 and baptised in Herne Bay, Kent, England on 3 Oct 1869. He married Annie Shaw there at Christ Church on 23 Jun 1904.

During his life, he was living on Salts Lane in Herne Bay on 2 Apr 18714 and at 3 Kings Road in Herne Bay on 2 Apr 19115.
