A boatman and the child of Abraham Mount (a mariner) and Mary Colyer (a charwoman), Edwin Mount, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England on Aug 4, 18691,2,3 and baptized in Herne Bay, Kent, England on Oct 3, 1869. He married Annie Shaw there at Christ Church on Jun 23, 1904.

During his life, he was living on Salts Lane in Herne Bay on Apr 2, 18714; and at 3 Kings Road in Herne Bay on Apr 2, 19115.
