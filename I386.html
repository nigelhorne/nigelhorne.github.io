The child of Henry Peters and Mary Scott, Henry Peters, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England in Apr 17811 and married Mary Standing (with whom he had 6 children: Henry Warman, John, Sarah Ann, Ann, Stephen and Rachel) at SS Mary & Eanswith in Folkestone on 28 May 18032.

During his life, he was living in Richibucto, Kent, New Brunswick, Canada in 1861.

He died on 12 Mar 1867 in Richibucto and was buried there at Methodist Point Cemetery after 12 Mar 1867.
