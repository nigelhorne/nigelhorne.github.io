The son of Henry Peters and Mary Scott, Henry Peters, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England in Apr 17811 and married Mary Standing (with whom he had 6 children: Henry Warman, John, Sarah Ann, Ann, Stephen and Rachel) at SS Mary & Eanswith in Folkestone on May 28, 18032.

Throughout his life, he lived in Richibucto, Kent, New Brunswick, Canada in 1861.

He died on Mar 12, 1867 in Richibucto and was buried there at Methodist Point Cemetery after Mar 12, 1867.
