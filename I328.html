Henry John Cowell 1867 - c1956

A shepherd and laborer in cement works and the son of John Cowell (a laborer) and Caroline Twyman, Henry Cowell, the first cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Wickhambreaux, Kent, England on Nov 26, 1867<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6,7</span>, was baptized there on Dec 29, 1867 and married Charlotte Mullen (with whom he had 3 children: <a href="I1175.html">Caroline Grace</a>, <a href="I1176.html">Lilly Clara</a> and <a href="I1174.html">John William</a>) in Malling, Kent, England around May 1890<span class="citation">9</span> (Apr/May/Jun).</p><p>Henry spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: on London Road, Ditton on Apr 2, 1871<span class="citation">10</span>; on Orchard Terrace, New Hythe Road, East Malling on Apr 3, 1881<span class="citation">11</span>; on Lunsford Lane, Lemsford on Apr 5, 1891<span class="citation">12</span>; in Larkfield on Mar 31, 1901<span class="citation">13</span>; at 3 Illogan Terrace, Larkfield on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">14</span>; at Illogan Terrace, Larkfield on Jun 19, 1921<span class="citation">15</span>; and at 3 Illogan Terrace, Larkfield on Sep 29, 1939<span class="citation">6</span> following the death of his wife in Mar 1931. Henry In 1921 he was working at George Eagleton Farmer in East Malling, Kent, England.<p>He died <i>c.</i> May 1956 in Maidstone, Kent<span class="citation">8</span>.
