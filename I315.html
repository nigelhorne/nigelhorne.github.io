Emma Thunder, the wife of James Henry Spratt (the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Herne, Kent, England c. Aug 18531 and baptized there at St Martin on Sep 4, 1853. She married James (an agricultural laborer) at St Martin, Kentish Town, London, England on Jan 1, 18853.
