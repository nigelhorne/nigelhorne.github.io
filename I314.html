Adeline Janet Horne (née Kewley) 1879 - 1958

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Adeline Kewley, the wife of Edmund Horne (the great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Lezayre, Ayre, Isle of Man on Sep 22, 18791,2. She married Edmund (a gardner) at Anglican Saint Martin, Montreal, Quebec, Canada on Jun 4, 19133.

Throughout her life, Adeline lived in several places: at Board School, Lezayre, Isle Of Man on Apr 3, 18814; in Quebec in 19015; in St Antoine, Quebec in 19111; and in Saint-Hubert, Longueuil, Quebec on Jun 1, 19212. She arrived in Canada in 1903.

She died in 1958 in Canada and was buried at Les Jardins Commémoratifs Lakeview Memorial Gardens, Quebec after 1958.
