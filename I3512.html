The child of William Peters and Ann Andrews, Harriet Peters, the first cousin four-times-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Cheriton, Kent, England c. 1815, was baptised in Folkestone, Kent, England on 30 Apr 1815 and married Joseph Hart (with whom she had 4 children: Henry, Sarah Anne, Jane and Elizabeth) at St Lawrence, Hougham, Kent, England on 6 Oct 18321. She arrived in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia on 1 Sept 1839 (sS Cornwall).

She died c. 1854 in New South Wales, Australia and was buried in Cumberland, New South Wales, Australia on 14 Aug 1854.
