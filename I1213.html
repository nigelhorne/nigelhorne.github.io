The 2nd of 12 children of Thomas Bixler and Cora Bufford, Ralph Bixler, the fourth cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Fort Wayne, Allen, Indiana, USA on 30 Sept 18961,2,3 and was married 3 times - to Nellie Parrish (on 29 Sept 1919 in Owen, Indiana, USA) Reva Butler (on 30 Apr 1932 in Putnam, Indiana, USA) Eva Miller (on 28 Jun 1945 in Martinsville, Morgan, Indiana)2. He had 9 children: Robert Edward, Ruth Marie, Ella Louise, Pauline, Katherine and Anna Mae with Nellie May; Joe with Reva Leealta; and Manson Kay with Eva Bell, and one surviving child. Like his father, he was a farmer.

During his life, he was living at his birthplace on 9 Jun 1900; in Washington, Indiana on 21 Apr 19104; at Joe Zink Road, Clay, Indiana on 3 Apr 19305 (the same place as his father had been living on 2 Apr 1930); in Morgan, Indiana on 9 May 1940 and in 1942; and at Pea Ridge Road, Spencer, Indiana in 1958.

He died on 9 Dec 1963 at Veterans Administration Hospital, Indianapolis, Marion, Indiana3 (pneumonitis) and was buried at New Union Cemetery, Spencer, Indiana on 11 Dec 1963 (a cold day with snow).
