The child of Joseph Hart and Harriet Peters, Sarah Hart, the second cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born c. 1836, was baptised in Folkestone, Kent, England on 7 Apr 1837 was married 3 times - to Peter Faucett (on 14 Sept 1853 in Penrith, New South Wales, Australia) William Hillier (on 9 Jul 1878 in Mudgee, New South Wales, Australia) William Marsh (on 10 Jan 1895 in Penrith). She had 1 child with Peter Faucett, Swanston. She arrived in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia on 1 Sept 1839.

She died on 4 Mar 1909.
