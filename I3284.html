An errand boy and bricklayer and the child of Frederick Goddard (a plumber's labourer) and Charlotte Page (a laundress), Samuel Goddard, the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England on 3 Feb 18751,2,3,4,5, was baptised there at Christ Church on 21 Mar 1875 and married Elizabeth Fuller (with whom he had 6 children: Winifred, Ellen, Hilda, Bertram, Sidney and Lillian, along with 2 surviving children) at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent, England on 11 Oct 18966.

During his life, he was living at Foord Road in Folkestone on 3 Apr 18811 and on 5 Apr 18912 and on Marshall Street in Folkestone in 1934.

He died on 1 Apr 1940 in Elham, Kent, England3.
