An invalid and the daughter of George Horne (a barman) and Rosa Horn, Alice Horne, the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England on Feb 15, 18931,2,3,4,5,6,7 and baptized there at St Paul's Church, King Street on May 26, 1897, the same day as her sister Emma Elizabeth.
