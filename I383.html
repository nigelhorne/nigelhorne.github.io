Notes

Henry Peters was born in Sussex, England and married Mary Scott in Kent, England. Henry and Mary had at least nine children born between 1781 and 1797 in Folkestone, Kent, England. Their daughters, Sarah and Mary, married two men by the last name of Tutt, possibly brothers. According to available records the Peters family all stayed in the Folkestone, Kent area of England and raised their families there. However, their oldest son, Henry, along with his wife and family leftfor New Brunswick, Canada, probably in the early 1830's. That was about the time that Henry Johnstone is reported to have brought the Kinread and Peters families to that area of Canada (Richibucto, Buctouche, Westmorland areas).
