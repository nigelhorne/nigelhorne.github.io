The child of James Spratt (an agricultural laborer) and Sarah SpainMary Spratt, the great-great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England c. Nov 18431,2,3,4,5 and baptized there on Dec 17, 1843.

Mary spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Crump's Farm in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Mar 30, 18517; at The Row in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 7, 18612; and at Parish Cottage in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 2, 18719 and on Apr 3, 18813 (when she was living with her parents).
