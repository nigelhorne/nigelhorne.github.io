The child of John Cowell (a yeoman) and Alice Troward, Joseph Cowell, the six times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born c. 1721 and baptized in Margate, Kent, England on Mar 30, 1722.

He died on Feb 28, 17481 and was buried at St John the Baptist Church in Margate on Mar 4, 17482.
