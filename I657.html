John Neame 1518 - 1577

The child of William Neame and Elizabeth Rolles, John Neame, the thirteen times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Woodnesborough, Kent, England in 1518. He was married to Joane Mockett. On Jul 26, 1574 he was granted a coat of arms.

He died in 1577 in Woodnesborough1 and was buried there on Apr 10, 15772.
