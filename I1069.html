Henry William Horne cFeb 1872 - 1922

A furniture porter and the child of George Horn (a brewery laborer) and Martha Wellard, Henry Horne, the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England c. Feb 18721,2,3,4,5,6,7,8 and baptized there at St George's Church on May 14, 1872.

Henry spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at 76 Queen Street in Ramsgate on Apr 3, 188110 and in 18912 (the same place as his parents had been living on Apr 2, 1871); at Paradise in Ramsgate on Mar 31, 190111; at 23 Belle Vue Cottages in Ramsgate on Apr 2, 191112; at 29 Sussex Street in Ramsgate on Jun 19, 19215; and on 2 Ethelbert Road in Ramsgate in 1922. Henry In 1921 he was working at R E Hodgman at 30 Kings Street, Ramsgate, Kent, England.

He died on Apr 20, 1922 in Ramsgate9.
