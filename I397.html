Ann Peters (née Andrews) c1795 - 1865

Ann Andrews, the wife of William Peters (the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Lyminge, Kent, England c. 17951,2,3, was baptized in Folkestone, Kent, England on Jan 18, 1795 and also married William (with whom she had 1 child, Harriet) there, on Feb 6, 18135.

Throughout her life, Ann lived in several places: in Folkestone in 18411; in Cheriton, Kent, England in 18512; and in Standford, Kent, England in 18613.

She died on Mar 14, 1865 in Lyminge4 (old Age) and was buried in Folkestone on Mar 18, 18655.
