Capt Albert Goodrich Peters 1865 - 1926

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A senior boat pilot and the 3rd of 4 children of Henry Peters and Elizabeth Cook, Albert Peters, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in New Brunswick, Canada on Nov 4, 1865<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9</span> and married Mary Hogan (with whom he had 2 children: <a href="I1149.html">Albert Ernest</a> and <a href="I1148.html">Margaret Celena</a>, along with 1 surviving child) in New Westminster, British Columbia, Canada on Sep 12, 1896<span class="citation">10</span>.</p><p>Throughout his life, Albert lived in several places: in Richibucto, Kent, New Brunswick in 1871<span class="citation">1</span> and in 1881<span class="citation">2</span>; in New Westminster on Apr 22, 1891<span class="citation">3</span>, in 1901<span class="citation">4</span> (the same place as his parents had been living in 1891) and in 1911<span class="citation">5</span>; and at 511 Ninth Street, British Columbia on Jun 1, 1921<span class="citation">6</span>. <p>He died on Feb 4, 1926 in New Westminster<span class="citation">7,8</span> and was buried there at Fraser Cemetery after Feb 4, 1926<span class="citation">8</span>.
