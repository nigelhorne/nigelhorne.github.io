An agricultural labourer and the child of Isaac Sargent and Rachel Vanwey, Alvin Sargent, the third cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Hanna, LaPorte, Indiana, USA on 21 Dec 18791,2,3,4 and married Daisy Eyler (with whom he had 3 children: Mervin Charles, Glen Russel and Bessie Irene) in Osceola, Polk, Nebraska, USA on 1 Aug 1899.

During his life, he was living at his birthplace on 8 Jun 1880; in Valley, Nebraska on 2 Jun 1885; in Canada, Nebraska on 14 Jun 1900 (the same place as his father was living in 1900), on 20 Apr 19105, on 9 Jan 1920, on 12 Apr 19306 and on 20 Apr 19407; and in Shelby, Nebraska in 1942. He served in the military from 1917 to 1918 (world War I Draft Registration Cards).

He died on 5 Jan 1950 in Polk1,2,3 and was buried at Graceland Cemetery, Shelby, Nebraska after 5 Jan 19501,3.
