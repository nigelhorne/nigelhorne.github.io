A coal agricultural merchants lorry driver and the child of Alfred Cowell (a transport driver) and Fanny Austen, William Cowell, the first cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Upper Stourmouth, Kent, England on 8 Jun 19091,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised in West Stourmouth, Kent, England on 11 Jul 1909 was married twice - to Florence Studham (c. Aug 1931 in Thanet, Kent, England) Doris Gambrill (c. Aug 1953 in Thanet, following the death of Florence May on 8 Oct 1951)7. He had 3 children with Florence Studham: Frances M, William Albert John and Ellen.

During his life, he was living at his birthplace on 2 Apr 191110; at Durlock Farm Cottages, Minster in Thanet on 19 Jun 19212; at 2 Mill Row, Birchington, Kent in 19368 and on 29 Sept 19393; at Mill Row, Birchington, Kent in 1941; and at 47 Park Lane, Birchington, Kent in 19559.

He died in Oct/Nov/Dec 1997 in Thanet5.
