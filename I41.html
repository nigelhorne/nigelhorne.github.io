Lilian Florence Hudson (née Lawrence) 1908 - 1976

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The daughter of Emily Lawrence, Lilian Lawrence, (also known as Lillian Cowell) the aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on Jan 29, 19081,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there on Feb 21, 1908 and also married William Hudson (a milkman with whom she had 1 child, Daphne Patricia) there at St Mary the Virgin Church, on Jun 11, 19277.

Lilian spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: on 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 2, 19119, when she was living with her brothers, Charles John and Frederick James; at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Jun 19, 19212; at Ebbsfleet Farm Cottages, Ebbsfleet on Sep 29, 19393; on Ebbsfleet Farm Cottages, Ebbsfleet Lane, Ebbsfleet in 1952; and at 4 Ebbsfleet Farm Cottages, Ebbsfleet Lane, Ebbsfleet in 19638.

She died on Apr 15, 1976 in Minster5.
