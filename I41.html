The daughter of Emily Lawrence, Lilian Lawrence, (also known as Lillian Cowell) the aunt of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on Jan 29, 1908<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6</span>, was baptized there on Feb 21, 1908 and also married William Hudson (a milkman with whom she had 1 child, Daphne Patricia) there at St Mary the Virgin Church, on Jun 11, 1927<span class="citation">7</span>.</p><p>Lilian spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: on 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">9</span>; at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Jun 19, 1921<span class="citation">2</span>; at Ebbsfleet Farm Cottages, Ebbsfleet on Sep 29, 1939<span class="citation">3</span>; on Ebbsfleet Farm Cottages, Ebbsfleet Lane, Ebbsfleet in 1952; and at 4 Ebbsfleet Farm Cottages, Ebbsfleet Lane, Ebbsfleet in 1963<span class="citation">8</span>. <p>She died on Apr 15, 1976 in Minster<span class="citation">5</span>.
