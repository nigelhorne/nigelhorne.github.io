The child of William Spratt (a labourer) and Mary Hughes, Sarah Spratt, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England c. 1821, was baptised there on 14 Oct 1821 and also married William Forman (with whom she had 1 child, Thomas) there on 12 Oct 18451.

During her life, she was living at The Street in St Nicholas-at-Wade on 6 Jun 18412 and at Pepper Alley in St Nicholas-at-Wade on 30 Mar 18513.

She died on 12 Mar 1865 in New Plymouth, Taranaki, New Zealand and was buried there at Te Henui after 12 Mar 1865.
