A private secretary wine & spirit merchant-ltd and the child of Henry Warner (a domestic under gardener) and Ada Ashfield, Elsie Warner, the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on 21 Jun 19071,2,3. She went to Bedford College, London, England
