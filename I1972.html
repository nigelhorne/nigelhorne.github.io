Stephen Page 1817 - 1900

An agricultural laborer and the child of Thomas Page (a laborer) and Caroline Marsh, Stephen Page, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Newington, Kent, England in 18171,2,3, was baptized in Newington, Hythe, Kent, England on Jan 26, 1817 and married Mary Butcher (with whom he had 7 children: William Richard, James, Charlotte Ann, Stephen, Mary, John and Sarah Elizabeth, along with 1 surviving child) at Ss Mary & Eanswith, Folkestone, Kent, England on Aug 13, 18375.

During his life, he was living in Folkestone on Jun 6, 18416; on Gibraltar Lane in Folkestone on Mar 30, 18511; at Bank House, Cheriton Road, Cheriton, Kent on Apr 3, 18812; and at Bank Cottage, Cheriton, Kent in 1900.

He died on Jan 2, 1900 in Elham, Kent, England3,4 and was buried at Folkestone Cemetery, Cheriton Road in Folkestone on Jan 8, 19004.
