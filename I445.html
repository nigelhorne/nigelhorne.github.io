A dressmaker and the child of John Horn (a labourer) and Mary Ann, Mary Horn, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England c. 18221. She married Henry Jayes at St John The Baptist Church in Margate on 30 Mar 18572.

During her life, she was living at Chapel Hill in Margate on 6 Jun 18414 and on 30 Mar 18513.

She died in 1880 in Thanet, Kent, England.
