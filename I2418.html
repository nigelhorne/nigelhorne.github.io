The son of Albert Matthews and Isabella Horn, Arthur Matthews, the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England on Oct 31, 1892<span class="citation">1,2,3,4</span>, was baptized there at Christ Church on Jan 10, 1893 and married Alice Thorpe (with whom he had 1 child, Stanley George) in Thanet, Kent, England around Feb 1921<span class="citation">6</span>. Like his father, he was a mariner. </p><p>Arthur spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: on Royal Road in Ramsgate on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">1</span>; at 40 Hertford Street in Ramsgate in 1935 and in 1936; at 1 Spring Villas in Ramsgate on Sep 29, 1939<span class="citation">2</span>; and at 31 Albert Street in Ramsgate in 1955<span class="citation">7</span> and in 1965<span class="citation">8</span>. He served in the military from 1914 to 1918 (<em>service number: SD 532</em>).<p>He died <i>c.</i> May 1967 in Thanet<span class="citation">5</span>.
