A smack owners's assistant and the child of Charles Spratt (a smack owner and fish salesman) and Susannah Thunder, Sidney Spratt, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England c. Aug 18821,2. He was married to Mary A. On 31 Mar 1901, he was living at 44 Addington Street in Ramsgate1.

He died on 28 Jun 1917 in Flanders, Belgium3,4,5 (died of wounds) and was buried at Lijssenthoek Military Cemetery, Poperinge, Arrondissement Ieper, West Flanders, Belgium after 28 Jun 19176.
