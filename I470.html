William Spain 1808 - 1891

An agricultural labourer and the child of John Spain (a labourer) and Susanna Newport, William Spain, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England on 30 Aug 18081,2,3,4,5, was baptised there at All Saints on 11 Sept 1808 and married Mary Mantle (with whom he had 4 children: Thomas, Charles, Mary Ann and William, along with 2 surviving children) at St Nicholas, Ash, Kent, England on 12 Oct 18367.

During his life, he was living on Knell Lane in Ash on 6 Jun 184112, at New Street in Ash on 30 Mar 18519, in Ash in 18612 and in 18713, and at New Street in Ash on 3 Apr 188110 and on 5 Apr 189111.

He died on 22 Jun 1891 in Eastry, Kent, England6 and was buried at St Nicholas on 26 Jun 18918.
