The child of Edward Bennet and ElizabethWilliam Bennet, the 4 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Selling, Kent, England c. 17731,2, was baptized there on Jan 24, 1773 and married Rebecca Trice (with whom he had 2 children: Rebecca and John) in Wickhambreaux, Kent, England on Mar 4, 17944 (KFHS CD 22).
