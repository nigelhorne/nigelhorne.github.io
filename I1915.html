Albert Thomas Cowell cMay 1894 - 1916

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Census Index ] | [ Family History ]

A colliery cabin man and the child of Albert Cowell (an agricultural laborer) and Ellen Young, Albert Cowell, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Dambridge, Kent, England c. May 18941.

During his life, he was living at Adisham Street, Adisham, Kent, England on Mar 31, 19014; and at Church Street, Nonington, Kent on Apr 2, 19115. He served in the army from Aug 26, 1914 to Mar 19, 1916 (regiment: Buffs (East Kent Regiment); Service number: GS/EK/319).

He died on Mar 19, 1916 at Bethune Hospital, Bethune, Hauts-de-France, France2,3 (died of wounds) and was buried at Bethune Town Cemetery, Bethune on Mar 20, 1916.
