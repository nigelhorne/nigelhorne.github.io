John Culmer c1804 - 1871

An agricultural laborer and the child of John Culmer (a parish clerk) and Sarah HawkesJohn Culmer, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England c. 18041,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there on Apr 1, 1804 and married Phoebe Port (with whom he had 8 children: Charles, George, Harriet, Emma, Jane, Esther, John and Mary, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mary the Virgin Church, Fordwich, Kent, England on Sep 9, 18207.

John spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Blean, Kent, England on Jun 6, 18419; at Doll House in Blean on Mar 30, 185110; and on New Road in Blean on Apr 7, 186111 and on Apr 2, 187112.

He died on Jul 2, 1871 in Blean5 (stomach Cancer) and was buried there at Ss Cosmus & Damian on Jul 9, 18718.
