Bertie Charles Cowell 1888 - 1963

A baker's roundsman, wholesale provision salesman & lorry driver and assistant house keeper and the youngest of 4 children of John Cowell (a police constable) and Emma Mills, Bertie Cowell, the third cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Tooting, London, England on 5 Mar 18881 and baptised in Upper Tooting, London, England on 6 May 1888. He married Ethel Carter in Willesden, London, England around Aug 19223.

During his life, he was living at 71 Victoria Road in Willesden on 5 Apr 18917; at 28 Claremont Road, Kilburn, London on 31 Mar 19018, on 2 Apr 19116 and on 19 Jun 19219 (when he was living with his); at 148 Priory Park Road in Willesden in 19344 and in 19365; and at 65 Princes Avenue, Wembley, London on 29 Sept 19391. In 1921 he was working at C Wilson & Co Egg Merchants at -, -.

He died in 1963 in Ealing, London, England2 and was buried at St Marylebone Cemetery, Westminster, London on 3 Apr 1963 (a drizzly day).
