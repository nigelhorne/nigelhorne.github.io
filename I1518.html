An army ordnance depot foreman and the child of Benjamin Cowell (a mariner) and Sarah Ewen (a needlewoman), George Cowell, the second cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England on Aug 25, 18601, was baptized there at St George's Church, Church Hill on Nov 25, 1860 and married Ellen Bockham (with whom he had 3 children: Alice Sarah, Ellen Martha and Elizabeth Mabel) in St. Mary's Church, Chatham, Kent, England on Apr 8, 18833.

During his life, he was living at Belle Vue Hill in Ramsgate on Apr 7, 18619; at 2 Rodney Street in Ramsgate on Apr 2, 18717; at Maidstone Road, Rochester, Kent on Apr 5, 18916; at 1 Quarters, Fort Clarence, Rochester, Kent on Mar 31, 19015; and at 186 Castle Road in Chatham on Sep 29, 19398 which is where he died c. Aug 19422. On Nov 12, 1878 he was serving in the navy (Royal Navy).He was buried at Maidstone Road Cemetery, Chatham on Sep 26, 19424.
