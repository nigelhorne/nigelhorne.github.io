Ellen May Kemp (née Cowell) 1904 - 1993

The daughter of Troward Cowell (a horseman) and Ann DennettEllen Cowell, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Birchington, Kent, England on May 12, 19041,2,3,4,5 and married Bert Kemp (a farm bailiff & tractor driver with whom she had 2 children: Joyce Margaret A and Mavis M, along with 1 surviving child) in Thanet, Kent, England around Nov 19277.

Ellen spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Gore End Farm in Birchington on Apr 2, 19118; at Granville House, Acol on Jun 19, 19219; at 6 Woodchurch Cottages, Margate on Sep 29, 19392; and on Hook Green Road, Southfleet in 1993.

She died on Jan 7, 1993 in Gravesend, Kent, England4,6.
