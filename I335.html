Ellen May Kemp (née Cowell) 1904 - 1993

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The daughter of Troward Cowell (a horseman) and Ann Dennett, Ellen Cowell, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Birchington, Kent, England on May 12, 1904<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5</span> and married Bert Kemp (a farm bailiff & tractor driver with whom she had 2 children: <a href="I2753.html">Joyce Margaret A</a> and <a href="I2754.html">Mavis M</a>, along with 1 surviving child) in Thanet, Kent, England around Nov 1927<span class="citation">7</span>.</p><p>Ellen spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Gore End Farm in Birchington on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">8</span>; at Granville House, Acol on Jun 19, 1921<span class="citation">9</span>; at 6 Woodchurch Cottages, Margate on Sep 29, 1939<span class="citation">2</span>; and on Hook Green Road, Southfleet in 1993. <p>She died on Jan 7, 1993 in Gravesend, Kent<span class="citation">4,6</span>.
