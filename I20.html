Charles John Cowell 1911 - 1956

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A lorry driver and the child of James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company) and Emily Lawrence, Charles Cowell, the uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 4 Jan 19111,2,3,4,5, was baptised in Ramsgate, Kent, England on 5 Feb 1911 and married Edith Bailey (with whom he had 2 children: Trevor John and Barrie Ernest) at St Mary the Virgin's Church, Minster, Thanet, Kent, England around May 19367 (Apr/May/Jun).

During his life, he was living at 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on 2 Apr 19119; at 7 Pouces Cottages, Manston, Kent on 19 Jun 19212; at Pouces Cottages, Manston, Kent on 10 Dec 1927; at Sunnymeade, Monkton Road, Minster in Thanet on 29 Sept 19393; and at 73 Monkton Road, Minster in Thanet in 19558 and in 1956.

He died on 7 Feb 1956 at Ramsgate General Hospital, West Cliff Road in Ramsgate4,6 (value of estate £681:13:0. Cancer of the bone marrow).
