Charles John Cowell 1911 - 1956

A truck driver and the son of James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company) and Emily LawrenceCharles Cowell, the uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Jan 4, 19111,2,3,4,5, was baptized in Ramsgate, Kent, England on Feb 5, 1911 and married Edith Bailey (with whom he had 2 children: Trevor John and Barrie Ernest) at St Mary the Virgin's Church, Minster, Thanet, Kent, England around May 19367 (Apr/May/Jun).

Charles spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: on 13 Ashburnham Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 191110; at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Jun 19, 19212; at Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Dec 10, 1927; on Sunnymeade, Monkton Road, Minster in Thanet on Sep 29, 19393; and on 73 Monkton Road, Minster in Thanet in 19539, in 19558 and in 1956.

He died on Feb 7, 1956 on Ramsgate General Hospital, West Cliff Road in Ramsgate4,6 (value of estate £681:13:0. Cancer of the bone marrow).
