A horseman on a farm and horseman and the child of Henry Beake (a waggoner on a farm) and Ellen Gibbs, William Beake, the second cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 1 Sept 19011,2,3,4 and baptised there on 3 Nov 1901.

During his life, he was living at Hale Cottages, The Street, St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on 2 Apr 19111; at Down Barton Farm in St Nicholas-at-Wade on 19 Jun 19215 and on 29 Sept 19392 (the same place as his parents had been living on 19 Jun 1921); and at 15 South Bush Lane, Rainham, Kent in 1961. In 1921 he was working at William Smith Farmer at Down Barton Farm, St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England.
