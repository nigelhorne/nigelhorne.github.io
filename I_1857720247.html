A confectioner: 254 king street: importer of french confectionary and toys and the child of Rene Marion (a baker) and Anne Corgniet (a tailoress), John Marion, the four times great-uncle of Michele Copp (née Phillips), was born in Charleston, South Carolina on Nov 6, 18121,2,3 and was married twice - to Ellen Haily Helene. He had 9 children: John F, Estelle, Margaret Josephine, Albert, Mary Ellen "Dolly", Florence Catherine, Effingham and Anna Alvinia (Elvinia) with Ellen; and Pauline Elizabeth with Helene.
