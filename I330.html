Frederick Troward Cowell 1878 - c1950

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

An agricultural labourer and the child of John Cowell (a labourer) and Caroline Twyman, Frederick Cowell, the first cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in East Malling, Kent, England on 7 Mar 18781,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10 and baptised there at St James the Great Church, 2 The Grange on 21 Apr 1878.

During his life, he was living at Orchard Terrace, New Hythe Road in East Malling on 3 Apr 18811; on Lunsford Lane in East Malling on 5 Apr 189112; in Royal Navy on 31 Mar 190114; in China and East Indies, Royal Navy, England on 2 Apr 191113; and at London Road, Malling, Kent, England on 29 Sept 19395. He served in the navy from 26 Mar 1900 to 29 May 1919 (Royal Navy - service number 294967).

He died c. Nov 1950 in Maidstone, Kent, England8,11.
