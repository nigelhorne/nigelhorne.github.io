The child of Clement Bedingfield and Margaret Twyman, Alice Bedingfield, the 7 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on 20 Nov 16811 and married Thomas Castle (with whom she had 5 children: Elizabeth, Ann, Margaret, Clement and Elizabeth) at St. John the Baptist Church in Margate on 14 Sept 17012.

She died on 1 Dec 1745 in Margate and was buried there at St John the Baptist Church on 18 Dec 17453,4,5.
