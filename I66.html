A smack owner and fish salesman and uk merchant navy apprentice and the child of James Spratt (an agricultural laborer) and Sarah SpainCharles Spratt, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England c. May 18541,2,3,4, was baptized there on Jul 16, 1854 and married Susannah Thunder (with whom he had 3 children: Sidney Charles James, Edwin Percy and Herbert Jesse) at St Martin, Herne, Kent, England on Jul 3, 18807.

Charles spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at The Row in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Apr 7, 18619; at Way, Minster, Thanet on Apr 2, 187112; in Ramsgate, Kent, England on Apr 3, 188113; at 44 Addington Street in Ramsgate in 18998, on Mar 31, 190110, on Apr 2, 191111, in 1913 and in 1918; and on Sidney Villa, Reculver Road, Herne Bay in 1930 which is where he died on Dec 35,6. He was buried at Eddington Cemetery, Herne Bay on Dec 8, 1930.
