Charlotte Cowell (née Mullen) c1855 - 1931

Charlotte Mullen, the wife of Henry John Cowell (the first cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne), was born in Hollingbourne, Kent, England c. 18551,2,3,4 and married Henry (a shepherd with whom she had 3 children: Caroline Grace, Lilly Clara and John William) in Malling, Kent, England around May 18905 (Apr/May/Jun).

Charlotte spent all of her life in Kent in England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: on Lunsford Lane, East Malling on 5 Apr 1891; in Larkfield, Kent, England on 31 Mar 19017; at 3 Illogan Terrace in Larkfield on 2 Apr 19113, when she was living with her son John William, following the death of her husband c. May 1956; and at Illogan Terrace, Larkfield, East Malling in Malling in 19216 and on 19 Jun 19219.

She died in Mar 1931 in Malling4.
