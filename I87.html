Troward Cowell 1791 - 1871

An agricultural laborer and the youngest of 7 children of Troward Cowell and Sarah DoughtyTroward Cowell, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on May 15, 17911,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on May 29, 1791 and married Mary Castle (with whom he had 9 children: Sarah, Mary Ann, Charlotte, William, Troward, Ann, Hannah, Jane and John, along with 1 surviving child) in Womenswold, Kent, England on Feb 6, 18197 (St. Margaret's Church according to IGI).

Troward spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Wingham, Kent, England in 18199; in Wingham Well, Kent, England on Jun 6, 184110; in Wingham in 18512; at Wingham Street in Wingham on Apr 7, 186111; and in Wingham on Apr 2, 18714.

He died on Dec 14, 1871 on Union Workhouse, Mill Lane, Eastry, Kent5,6 (apoplexy) and was buried at St Mary the Virgin Church, Nonington, Kent on Dec 20, 18718.
