Troward Cowell 1791 - 1871

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

An agricultural labourer and the youngest of 7 children of Troward Cowell and Sarah Doughty, Troward Cowell, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on 15 May 17911,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 29 May 1791 and married Mary Castle (with whom he had 9 children: Sarah, Mary Ann, Charlotte, William, Troward, Ann, Hannah, Jane and John, along with 1 surviving child) in Womenswold, Kent, England on 6 Feb 18197 (St. Margaret's Church according to IGI).

During his life, he was living in Wingham, Kent, England in 18199; in Wingham Well, Kent, England on 6 Jun 184110; in Wingham in 18512; at Wingham Street in Wingham on 7 Apr 186111; and in Wingham on 2 Apr 18714.

He died on 14 Dec 1871 on Union Workhouse, Mill Lane, Eastry, Kent5,6 (apoplexy) and was buried at St Mary the Virgin Church, Nonington, Kent on 20 Dec 18718.
