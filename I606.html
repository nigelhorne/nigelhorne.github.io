Elizabeth Hawkes (née Godden) 1756 - 1793

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Elizabeth Godden, the 5 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Ash, Kent, England in 1756 and married William Hawkes (with whom she had 1 child, Sarah Anne) in Ash on Aug 16, 17771, which is also where she died in 1793.
