Harriet Allen Horn (née Fox) 1839 - 1926

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Harriet Fox, the wife of James Horn (the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 13 May 18391,2,3, was baptised there on 23 Jun 1839 and married James (a brewer's labourer with whom she had 10 children: James, Maria L, Rosa Louisa, Isabella, Thomas G, Alice, Charles, Arthur Albert, Emily and Pleasant Alice, along with 1 surviving child) at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent, England on 21 Apr 18604.

Throughout her life, Harriet lived in several places: at Hertford Cottage, Hertford Place, Ramsgate, Kent on 2 Apr 18717; on Chapel Road, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent on 3 Apr 18816; and at 67 Addington Street, Ramsgate, Kent on 31 Mar 19015 - less than a mile from her daughter Rosa Louisa Horn, grandson George Charles Horne and granddaughter Alice Rose Horne who were living on 62 Boundary Road, Ramsgate, Kent.

She died on 7 Feb 1926 on 9 Priory Road, Ramsgate, Kent.
