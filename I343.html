The son of Charles Culmer and Emma Beak, Charles Culmer, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England on Oct 18, 18791,2,3 and baptized in West Stourmouth, Kent, England on Nov 23, 1879. He married Rosetta Crump in Thanet, Kent, England around Nov 19295. Like his father, he was an agricultural laborer.

Charles spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at The Square in Stourmouth on Apr 3, 18816; in Westmarsh on Apr 5, 18917; at Newport Cottage, Lydden, Garlinge on Jun 19, 19218; at 4 Lydden Hill, Margate on Sep 29, 19392; and on Rosemead, Maidstone Road, Horsmonden in 1958.
