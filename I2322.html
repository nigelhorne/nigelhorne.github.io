Nov 18511,2,3, was baptized there at St Mary on Feb 1, 1852 and also married John Knowler (a general laborer with whom she had 4 children: Arthur John, William Edward, Rosa Ellen and George, along with 1 surviving child) there at St Mary, on Oct 14, 18735.
