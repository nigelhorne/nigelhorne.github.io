Phoebe Culmer (née Port) c1797 - 1877

The child of James Port and Sarah Hoffman, Phoebe Port, the 3 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England c. 17971,2,3,4,5,6,7,8, was baptized there at St Martin on Jul 16, 1797 and married John Culmer (an agricultural laborer with whom she had 8 children: Charles, George, Harriet, Emma, Jane, Esther, John and Mary, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mary the Virgin Church, Fordwich, Kent, England on Sep 9, 18209.

Phoebe spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: in Blean on Jun 6, 18412; at Doll House, Blean on Mar 30, 18514; and on New Road, Blean on Apr 7, 18616 and on Apr 2, 187111.

She died on Sep 29, 1877 at Bretts Passage, Canterbury, Kent8 (chronic bronchitis and old age) and was buried at St Dunstan, Canterbury on Oct 4, 187710.
