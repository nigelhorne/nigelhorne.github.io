A cheesemonger and the child of John Cowell (a yeoman) and Alice Troward, Troward Cowell, the six times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England in 17141, was baptised there on 27 Jan 1715 and married Sarah Wilkins (with whom he had 2 children: Alice Troward and Troward) at St Peter-upon-Cornhill Church, Cornhill, London, England on 26 Jan 17381 (Civil marriage in All Hallows, Barking, then parish marriage the next day at St Peter upon Cornhill). In 1755, he was living at Tower Street, London, England.

He died in 1763 in London and was buried at All Hallows, Barking, London on 24 Sept 17632.
