Patricia Mary Light (née Cowell) cMay 1937 - 2013

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Frederick Cowell (a lorry driver) and Doris Maxted, Patricia Cowell, a cousin on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Thanet, Kent, England c. May 19371,2. She married Sidney Light (with whom she had 2 surviving children Neil M and Tina M) in Thanet around Aug 19573. On 29 Sept 1939, she was living at 5 Pouces Cottages, Manston, Kent, England - less than a mile from her aunt Joyce Cowell who was living at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet.

She died in Nov 2013 at 3 Stirling Way, Ramsgate, Kent and was buried in Thanet on 3 Dec 2013 (an overcast day).
