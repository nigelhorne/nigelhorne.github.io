Patricia Mary Light (née Cowell) cMay 1937 - 2013

The child of Frederick Cowell (a truck driver) and Doris MaxtedPatricia Cowell, a cousin on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Thanet, Kent, England c. May 19371,2. She married Sidney Light (with whom she had 2 surviving children Neil M and Tina M) in Thanet around Aug 19573. On Sep 29, 1939, she lived at 5 Pouces Cottages, Manston, Kent, England - less than a mile from her aunt Joyce Margaret Cowell who was living at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet.

She died in Nov 2013 at 3 Stirling Way, Ramsgate, Kent and was buried in Thanet on Dec 3, 2013 (an overcast day).
