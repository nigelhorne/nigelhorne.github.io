The child of William Page and Margaret Drake, Frederick Page, the second cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England c. Nov 18631,2, was baptised there at The Parish Church of St Mary and St Eanswythe, Church St on 6 Dec 1863 and also married Margaret Else (a charwoman with whom he had 7 children: Frederick William, James Walter, Emma Janet, Thomas Victor, Agnes Jean, Stephen Ernest and Sidney Arthur, along with 2 surviving children) there at The Parish Church of St Mary and St Eanswythe, Church St on 23 Jan 18876. Like his father, he was a bricklayer.
