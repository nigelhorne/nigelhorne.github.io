A charwork and the daughter of John Spratt (an agricultural laborer) and Sophia DaleElla Spratt, the great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Feb 2, 18951,2,3,4,5,6,7, was baptized there on Mar 31, 1895 and married Ernest Verrall (a window cleaner with whom she had 5 children: Cyril Ernest, Elsie Amy, Norman Charles, John Leslie and Arthur George) in Thanet, Kent, England around May 19228.

Ella spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: on 2 Nash Court Cottage, Nash Road, Margate on Mar 31, 190110, when she was living with her brother George Dale, sister May and on Apr 2, 191111; at Nash in Thanet on Jun 19, 19213; and on 74 Invicta Road, Margate on Sep 29, 19394, in 19699 and in 1971.

She died on Nov 10, 1971 in Bridge, Kent, England6.
