The child of Robert Peters and Elizabeth Kirk, Robert Peters, the 6 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Horsham, Sussex, England in 1691, was baptised in Cowfold, Sussex, England on 8 Oct 1691 and married Elizabeth Fuller (with whom he had 1 child, Ann) in Hastings, Sussex, England on 27 Nov 1716 (SFHG The Sussex Marriage Index (up to 1837). St. Clements according to IGI).

During his life, he was living in Oare, Sussex, England1.
