A stoker gas and lamplighter and the son of James Mount (a stoker at gasworks) and Mary Gammon (a laundress)Edward Mount, the second cousin three-times-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Herne, Kent, England on Mar 3, 18791,2, was baptized in Herne Bay, Kent, England on May 25, 1879 and also married Mabel Clarke (with whom he had 4 children: George, Edward, Ivan James Frederick and Francis John, along with 1 surviving child) there at Christ Church, on Oct 28, 19063.

Edward spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at 7 Salts Lane in Herne Bay on Apr 3, 18817; at 24 King Street in Herne Bay on Apr 5, 18916; at 49 King Street in Herne Bay on Mar 31, 19015; on 10 Arkley Road in Herne Bay in 19094; and at 46 King Street in Herne Bay on Apr 2, 19118 on the same road as his parents who were living at 49 King Street.
