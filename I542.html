The child of John Cowell (a yeoman) and Alice Troward, Jane Cowell, the six times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England c. 1711 and baptised there at St John the Baptist Church on 4 Jul 1711. She married Thomas Gurney there at St John the Baptist Church on 29 May 17321.

She died c. 1773 in Margate and was buried there at St John the Baptist Church on 4 Apr 17732,3.
