The child of John Cowell (a yeoman) and Alice Troward, Jane Cowell, the six times great-aunt of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Margate, Kent, England <i>c.</i> 1711 and baptized there at St John the Baptist Church on Jul 4, 1711.
