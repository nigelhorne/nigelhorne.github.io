The child of John Cowell (a yeoman) and Alice TrowardJane Cowell, the six times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England c. 1711 and baptized there at St John the Baptist Church on Jul 4, 1711. She married Thomas Gurney there at St John the Baptist Church, on May 29, 17321.

She died c. 1773 in Margate and was buried there at St John the Baptist Church on Apr 4, 17732,3.
