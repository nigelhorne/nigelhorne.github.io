The twin brother of Maureen Joyce and the child of Frederick Cowell (a lorry driver) and Doris Maxted, Melvin Cowell, a cousin on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Thanet, Kent, England on 9 Feb 19461,2,3 along with his twin sister Maureen Joyce and. He was orphaned at the age of 14 following the death of his father in 1953 and mother in 1960.

He married Linda Carter (with whom he had 3 surviving children Mark James, Graham Edward and Leigh Robert) at Ramsgate Register Office, Ramsgate, Kent, England around Nov 19674 In 1974, he was living at 32 Freeman's Road, Minster in Thanet5.

He died on 9 Aug 1994 in Greenwich, London, England3 (throat Cancer) and was buried on Minster Cemetery, Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet after 9 Aug 1994.
