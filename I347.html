William Deal, the husband of Sarah Ann Dale (the great-great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Barham, Kent, England c. 18481,2,3, was baptized there on Sep 24, 1848 and married Sarah (a caretaker of bridge union workhouse with whom he had 3 children: Austin Alfred, Ernest and May) at St Mary's Church, Chartham, Kent, England on Nov 11, 18756.

Throughout his life, William lived in several places: at Asylum Farm Cottages in Chartham on Apr 3, 18811; on Old Road in Chartham on Apr 5, 18912; and at 14 Asylum Cottages in Chartham on Mar 31, 19017.
