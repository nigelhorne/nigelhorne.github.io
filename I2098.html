Louisa Bennett (née Jordan) 1876 - 1968

The daughter of James Jordan (a horse keeper) and Julia CulmerLouisa Jordan, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Faversham, Kent, England on Jul 30, 18761,2 and married Charles Bennett (a brewer's drayman with whom she had 4 children: Charlotte Priscilla, Charles James, Frederick Arthur and Margaret D Elizabeth) at St Mary Of Charity in Faversham on Feb 23, 19014.

Louisa spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at 2 Mill Place in Faversham on Apr 3, 18815, when she was living with her brother, Frederick John Thomas; at Claremont Place, Canterbury, Kent, England on Mar 31, 19017 and on Apr 2, 19116; and at 4 Vicarage Street in Faversham on Sep 29, 19391 - less than a mile from her first cousin once-removed on her mother's side Alfred William Charles Coombs who was living at 60 Makenade Avenue in Faversham and her cousin on her mother's side Sidney John Coombs who was living on 11 Napleton Road in Faversham -.

She died on May 29, 1968 in Sittingbourne, Kent, England3.
