He married Elsie Punyer in Sittingbourne, Kent, England around Feb 19566.

Frederick spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at Claremont Place in Canterbury on Apr 2, 19111; at 4 Vicarage Street, Faversham in Jan 1939 - less than a mile from his second cousin on his mother's side Alfred William Charles Coombs who was living at 60 Makenade Avenue, Faversham, Kent, his second cousin on his mother's side Bertie Charles J Culmer, second cousin on his mother's side Harry Ronald Culmer, second cousin on his mother's side Maurice Stanley Allan Culmer, first cousin once-removed on his mother's side Harry Robert Culmer who were living on 79 St Marys Road, Faversham, Kent - and his first cousin once-removed on his mother's side Sidney John Coombs who was living on 11 Napleton Road, Faversham, Kent - and on Sep 29, 19392 (when he was living with his mother, Louisa); and at 2 Vicarage Street, Faversham in 1976.
