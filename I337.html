The son of Troward Cowell and Ann Dennett, George Cowell, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Northwood, Kent, England on Oct 27, 1894<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6,7</span>, was baptized in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Mar 3, 1895. He married Edith Wood (with whom he had 1 surviving child) on St James Church, Canterbury Road, Garlinge, Kent, England on Oct 6, 1928<span class="citation">8</span>. Like his father, he was a horseman. </p><p>George spent all of his life in Kent, England.
