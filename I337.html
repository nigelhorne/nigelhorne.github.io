The child of Troward Cowell and Ann Dennett, George Cowell, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Northwood, Kent, England on 27 Oct 18941,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on 3 Mar 1895. He married Edith Wood (with whom he had 1 surviving child, ) in Thanet, Kent, England around Nov 19287 (Oct/Nov/Dec). Like his father, he was a horseman.

During his life, he was living at Gore End Farm, Birchington, Kent on 31 Mar 19018; at The Elms, Sarre, Kent on 2 Apr 19112; at Granville House, Acol, Kent on 19 Jun 19219; and at Acol Hill Farm, Shottendane Road, Birchington, Kent on 29 Sept 19393 - less than a mile from his sister Ellen May who was living at 6 Woodchurch Cottages, Margate, Kent. In 1921 he was working at Charles Willett Farmer in Acol Farm, Kent. George appeared in a newspaper on 30 Sep 1988 (https://www.findmypast.com/image-viewer?issue=BL/0004721/19880930&page=0002&article=002&stringtohighlight=george%20cowell).
