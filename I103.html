Johanna Brooker Lawrence (née Edwards) 1831 - 1917

The child of Daniel Edwards (a laborer) and Johanna Brooker (a pauper char woman), Johanna Edwards, the great-great-grandmother of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England in 1831<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6,7</span>, was baptized there on Jan 30, 1831 and also married William Lawrence (a harbor laborer with whom she had 11 children: <a href="I140.html">George</a>, <a href="I146.html">Mary Carter</a>, <a href="I145.html">William</a>, <a href="I138.html">John</a>, <a href="I144.html">Annie</a>, <a href="I139.html">Elizabeth</a>, <a href="I143.html">Daniel</a>, <a href="I43.html">James</a>, <a href="I141.html">Hannah</a>, <a href="I142.html">Jane</a> and <a href="I147.html">Emily</a>, along with 2 surviving children) there, on Nov 22, 1851<span class="citation">9</span>.</p><p>Johanna spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at her birthplace on Jun 6, 1841<span class="citation">10</span> and in 1851<span class="citation">2</span> (the same place as her parents had been living in 1841); on Chapel Lane, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 7, 1861<span class="citation">11</span>; at Rear of Southwood Lodge, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 1871<span class="citation">12</span>; at 3 Providence Cottage, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 5, 1891<span class="citation">5</span>; on 3 Lorne Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Mar 31, 1901<span class="citation">13</span>, when she was living with her daughter Elizabeth following the death of her husband on Jun 11, 1898; and on 16 Bloomsbury Road, Ramsgate on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">14</span>. <p>She died on Jan 31, 1917 at 2 Mutrix Cottages, Garlinge, Kent<span class="citation">8</span> (senile decay).
