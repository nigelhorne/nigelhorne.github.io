A constable metropolitan police officer and the son of Thomas Horn (a farm bailiff) and Elizabeth Warner (a general shopkeeper)Harry Horn, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on 11 Aug 18641,2,3,4,5, was baptised there on 11 Dec 1864 andhad 6 children with Mary Jane Wood: Henry Lewis, Florence A, Alice M, Ethel M, Albert Victor and Arthur, along with 1 surviving child.
