Thomas Page c1790 - 1864

A labourer and the child of Thomas Page and Charlotte Johnson, Thomas Page, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Newington, Kent, England c. 17901,2,3,4, was baptised in Newington, Hythe, Kent, England on 30 Jan 1791 and married Caroline Marsh (with whom he had 2 children: Stephen and Caroline Bromley) in Folkestone, Kent, England on 13 Jul 18115.

During his life, he was living in Folkestone in 18411; and in Elham, Kent, England in 18512 and in 18613 which is where he died in Jan/Feb/Mar 18644 (not the Thomas Page who died 12/12/1868 - that one was a master mariner).
