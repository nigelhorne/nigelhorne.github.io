Thomas Page c1790 - 1864

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A laborer and the child of Thomas Page and Charlotte Johnson, Thomas Page, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Newington, Kent, England c. 17901,2,3,4, was baptized in Newington, Hythe, Kent, England on Jan 30, 1791 and married Caroline Marsh (with whom he had 2 children: Stephen and Caroline Bromley) in Folkestone, Kent, England on Jul 13, 18115.

Throughout his life, he lived in Folkestone in 18411; and in Elham, Kent in 18512 and in 18613 which is where he died in Jan/feb/mar 18644 (not the Thomas Page who died 12/12/1868 - that one was a master mariner).
