Rose Mary Cowell 1900 - 1973

A domestic and the daughter of Troward Cowell (a horseman) and Ann Dennett, Rose Cowell, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on Jan 21, 19001,2,3,4,5 and baptized there at St John the Baptist Church on Mar 4, 1900.

Rose spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Gore End Farm, Birchington, Kent, England on Mar 31, 19019 and on Apr 2, 191110; at Granville House, Acol on Jun 19, 192111; and on 20 Approach Road in Margate on Feb 16, 19738. Rose In 1921 she was working at Henry Woolley Private in Beadon Lodge, Margate.

She died on Feb 16, 1973 in Thanet, Kent3,4,6,7.
