The eldest of 3 children of Thomas Atkinson and Mary Warman, Dorcas Atkinson, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in New Brunswick, Canada on 4 May 1855 and married John Hutchison (with whom she had 2 children: Mary Jane and John Hedley) in Harcourt, Kent, New Brunswick on 2 Dec 1884.

She died on 14 Mar 1930 in Canada and was buried at Saint Andrews Presbyterian Cemetery, Harcourt, Kent, New Brunswick after 14 Mar 1930.
