Thomas Laurence c1808 - 1878

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Zechariah Lawrence (a labourer) and Ann Cooper, Thomas Laurence, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Ash, Kent, England c. 18081,2,3,4,5 and baptised there on 25 Sept 1808. He was married to Rebecca.

During his life, he was living at his birthplace in 18411, in 18512, in 18613 and in 18714 which is where he died on 30 Apr 18785,6. He was buried there on 3 May 18786.
