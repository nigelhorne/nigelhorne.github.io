The child of Zechariah Lawrence (a laborer) and Ann Cooper, Thomas Laurence, the three times great-uncle of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Ash, Kent, England <i>c.</i> 1808<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5</span> and baptized there on Sep 25, 1808. He was married to Rebecca.</p><p>Thomas spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived at his birthplace in 1841<span class="citation">1</span>, in 1851<span class="citation">2</span>, in 1861<span class="citation">3</span> and in 1871<span class="citation">4</span> which is where he died on Apr 30, 1878<span class="citation">5,6</span>.
