Thomas Laurence c1808 - 1878

The child of Zechariah Lawrence (a laborer) and Ann CooperThomas Laurence, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Ash, Kent, England c. 18081,2,3,4,5 and baptized there on Sep 25, 1808. He was married to Rebecca.

Thomas spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived at his birthplace in 18411, in 18512, in 18613 and in 18714 which is where he died on Apr 30, 18785,6. He was buried there on May 3, 18786.
