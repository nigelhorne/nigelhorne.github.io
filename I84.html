A farm waggoner and the child of John Culmer (an agricultural laborer) and Phoebe Port, Charles Culmer, the great-great-grandfather of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Hoath, Kent, England <i>c.</i> 1822<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6</span>, was baptized there on Dec 29, 1822 and married Charlotte Coleman (a pauper with whom he had 9 children: <a href="I92.html">Ann</a>, <a href="I91.html">Henry</a>, <a href="I94.html">Charles</a>, <a href="I93.html">Elizabeth</a>, <a href="I21.html">Emma</a>, <a href="I95.html">John</a>, <a href="I99.html">George</a>, <a href="I96.html">Jane</a> and <a href="I90.html">Frances</a>, along with 5 surviving children) in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on May 21, 1850<span class="citation">7</span> (St. Nicholas At Wade, Kent, England).</p><p>Charles spent all of his life in Kent, England.
