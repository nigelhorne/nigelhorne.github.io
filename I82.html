An agricultural labourer and farm labourer and the child of Thomas Poysden and SarahGeorge Poysden, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Sturry, Kent, England in 18011,2,3, was baptised in Canterbury, Kent, England on 18 Apr 1802 and married Sophia Chapple (with whom he had 3 children: Esther, Mary and Mary) at Ss Cosmus & Damian, Blean, Kent, England on 27 Dec 18245.

George spent all of his life in Kent in England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Harbledown, Kent, England in 18411; in Chartham, Kent, England in 18512; in Chartham Hatch, Kent, England on 30 Mar 18516; and in Chartham in 18613 which is where he died on 23 Jan 18654 (heart disease).
