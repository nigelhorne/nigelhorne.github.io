George Poysden 1801 - 1865

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

An agricultural laborer and farm laborer and the child of Thomas Poysden and Sarah, George Poysden, the 3 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Sturry, Kent, England in 18011,2,3, was baptized in Canterbury, Kent, England on Apr 18, 1802 and married Sophia Chapple (with whom he had 3 children: Esther, Mary and Mary) at Ss Cosmus & Damian, Blean, Kent, England on Dec 27, 18245.

George spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: in Harbledown in 18411; in Chartham in 18512; in Chartham Hatch on Mar 30, 18516; and in Chartham in 18613 which is where he died on Jan 23, 18654 (heart disease).
