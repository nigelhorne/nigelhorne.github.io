William Peters 1792 - 1843

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Henry Peters and Mary Scott, William Peters, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England in 17921,2,3,4,5,6, was baptized there at Parish Church of St Mary & St Eanswythe, Church St on Dec 9, 1792 and also married Ann Andrews (with whom he had 1 child, Harriet) there, on Feb 6, 181310.

Throughout his life, he lived in Woolwich, London, England on Sep 4, 18285; and in Australia in 18292. William appeared in court in Maidstone, Kent, England on Aug 7, 1828 (sentenced Transport to Australia). On Apr 18, 1829 he arrived in Sydney Cove, New South Wales, Australia.

He died on Nov 14, 1843 in Maitland, New South Wales, Australia6,7,8,9 and was buried Glebe Cemetery in Maitland on Nov 18, 18436.
