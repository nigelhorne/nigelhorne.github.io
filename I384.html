William Peters 1792 - 1843

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Henry Peters and Mary Scott, William Peters, the four times great-uncle of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England in 1792<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6</span>, was baptized there at Parish Church of St Mary & St Eanswythe, Church St on Dec 9, 1792 and also married Ann Andrews (with whom he had 1 child, Harriet) there, on Feb 6, 1813<span class="citation">10</span>.</p><p>Throughout his life, he lived in Woolwich, London, England on Sep 4, 1828<span class="citation">5</span>; and in Australia in 1829<span class="citation">2</span>. William appeared in court in Maidstone, Kent, England on Aug 7, 1828 (sentenced Transport to Australia). On Apr 18, 1829 he arrived in Sydney Cove, New South Wales, Australia.<p>He died on Nov 14, 1843 in Maitland, New South Wales, Australia<span class="citation">6,7,8,9</span> and was buried Glebe Cemetery in Maitland on Nov 18, 1843<span class="citation">6</span>.
