William Peters 1792 - 1843

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of Henry Peters and Mary ScottWilliam Peters, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England in 17921,2,3,4,5,6, was baptised there at Parish Church of St Mary & St Eanswythe, Church St on 9 Dec 1792 and also married Ann Andrews (with whom he had 1 child, Harriet, ) there, on 6 Feb 181310.

Throughout his life, he lived in Woolwich, London, England on 4 Sept 18285; and in Australia in 18292. On 18 Apr 1829 he arrived in Sydney Cove, New South Wales, Australia.William appeared in court in Maidstone, Kent, England on 7 Aug 1828 (sentenced Transport to Australia).

He died on 14 Nov 1843 in Maitland, New South Wales, Australia6,7,8,9 and was buried Glebe Cemetery in Maitland on 18 Nov 18436.
