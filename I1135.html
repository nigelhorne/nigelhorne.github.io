The 5th of 8 children of Richard Colpitts and Anne Peters, Henry Colpitts, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Pleasant Vale, Albert, New Brunswick, Canada on 19 Sept 18841,2,3,4,5, was baptised in Hillsborough, Albert, New Brunswick, Canada c. 1888 and married Mary Buchanan (with whom he had 4 children: William Hector, Harold Alistair, Donald Ralph and Priscilla Anne, along with 3 surviving children) in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada on 17 Apr 19127.

During his life, he was living in Elgin, Albert, New Brunswick, Canada in 18911 and in 19012 (the same place as his father had been living in 1891); and in Toronto, Manitoba c. 19163.

He died on 21 Mar 1975 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada4,6 and was buried at Glen Eden Cemetery, 4477 Main St, West Saint Paul, Manitoba on 24 Mar 1975 (a cold and overcast day) following his funeral at Bardal Funeral Home, Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.
