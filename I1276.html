Harriet Fox, the wife of James Horn (the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in St Peters, Thanet, Kent, England on May 13, 18391,2,3, was baptized there on Jun 23, 1839 and married James (a brewer's laborer with whom she had 11 children: James, Maria L, Rosa Louisa, Rosa Louisa, Isabella, Thomas G, Alice, Charles, Arthur Albert, Emily and Pleasant Alice, along with 1 surviving child) at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent, England on Apr 21, 18604.

During her life, she was living at Hertford Cottage, Hertford Place, Ramsgate, Kent on Apr 2, 18717; at Chapel Road, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent on Apr 3, 18816; and at 67 Addington Street, Ramsgate, Kent on Mar 31, 19015.

She died on Feb 7, 1926 at 9 Priory Road, Ramsgate, Kent.
