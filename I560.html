A gardener heavy and the older of 2 children of Isaac Horne (a gardener domestic servant) and Lucy Stockbridge (a dressmaker), Edward Horne, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Broadstairs, Kent, England on 12 Jun 19091,2,3,4,5 and married Hilda Dale (with whom he had 3 children: Anthony J, Martin Stuart and Gillian T, along with 1 surviving child) at Wesleyan Church in Broadstairs on 2 Jun 19326.
