The child of Martin Aucoin and Barbe Minguet, Michelle Aucoin, the 10 times great-grandmother of Michele Copp (née Phillips), was born in La Rochelle, Manche, Basse-Normandie, France in 16181, and was orphaned at the age of 14 following the death of her mother in 1631 and father in 1632.

She married Michel Boudrot (with whom she had 1 child, Marguerite) in Nova Scotia, Canada in 16401

She died on Dec 18, 1706 in Port Royal, , Nova Scotia.
