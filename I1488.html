A smack owners's assistant and the child of Charles Spratt (a smack owner and fish salesman) and Susannah ThunderSidney Spratt, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England c. Aug 18821,2. He was married to Mary A. On Mar 31, 1901, he lived at 44 Addington Street in Ramsgate1, when he was living with his brothers, Edwin Percy and Herbert Jesse. During 1917, he was serving in the Army in Flanders, Belgium (regiment: Royal Army Service Corps; Rank: Private; Service number: DM2/228350).

He died on Jun 28, 1917 in Flanders, Belgium3,4,5 (died of wounds) and was buried at Lijssenthoek Military Cemetery, Poperinge, Arrondissement Ieper, West Flanders, Belgium after Jun 28, 19176.
