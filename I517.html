The child of William Spratt (a labourer) and Mary Hughes, Matilda Spratt, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on 5 Jun 18251,2,3,4,5,6,7,8 and married John Collins (a shepherd with whom she had 6 children: William, John Elgar, Elizabeth Sarah, Ann, Ellen Jane and Ada Louisa, along with 3 surviving children) in St Nicholas-at-Wade on 30 Nov 184410.
