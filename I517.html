The daughter of William Spratt (a laborer) and Mary HughesMatilda Spratt, the three times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England on Jun 5, 18251,2,3,4,5,6,7,8 and married John Collins (a shepherd with whom she had 6 children: William, John Elgar, Elizabeth Sarah, Ann, Ellen Jane and Ada Louisa, along with 3 surviving children) in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Nov 30, 184410.

Matilda spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at The Street in St Nicholas-at-Wade on Jun 6, 184117; at Reynolds Farm, Chislet, Kent, England on Mar 30, 18512; on Prospect Road, Minster, Thanet on Apr 7, 186118; at Cottage, Birchington on Apr 2, 187115; at Evering Farm, Alkham on Apr 3, 188114; at Everden, Alkham on Apr 5, 189112; and at Ivy Cottage, Everden, Alkham on Mar 31, 190113.
