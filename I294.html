Elizabeth Emma Gill (née Cowell) 1906 - c1986

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A dressmaker and the 3rd of 4 children of Charles Cowell (a newsagent) and Elizabeth Russell, Elizabeth Cowell, the first cousin once-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Woodchurch, Thanet, Kent, England on Jun 4, 19061,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 and married Ernest Gill (a bricklayer with whom she had 2 children: Stella Patricia and Elizabeth) in Westhampnett, Sussex, England on Apr 9, 192811.

Throughout her life, Elizabeth lived in several places: at Streele Cottages, Pulborough, Sussex on Apr 2, 191113; on Full Cottages, Highfield Road, Bognor, West Sussex, England on Jun 19, 192114; at Conway, 24 Murina Avenue, Bognor Regis, West Sussex on Sep 29, 19392 on the same road as her parents who were living at Saint Stephen, Murina Ave; and at 24 Murina Avenue, Bognor Regis, West Sussex in 197512. Elizabeth In 1921 she was working at Miss Pennicott Dressmaker.

She died c. Feb 1986 in Chichester, Sussex4,5,10.
