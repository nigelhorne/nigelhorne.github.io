William Cowell c1829 - c1868

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

A carter and the child of Troward Cowell (an agricultural laborer) and Mary CastleWilliam Cowell, the three times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Wingham, Kent, England c. 18291,2,3,4,5, was baptized there on Jul 12, 1829 and married Hannah White (with whom he had 6 children: James, Albert, Emily Hannah, Sarah Ann, Susannah and William, along with 3 surviving children) in Bridge, Kent, England around Nov 18507.

Throughout his life, William lived in several places: at Wingham Well in Wingham on Jun 6, 18418; at his birthplace in 18512; and at Chalk Pit in Wingham on Apr 7, 18619.

He died c. Feb 1868 in Eastry, Kent, England4,5,6 and was buried in Wingham on Mar 26, 18685.
