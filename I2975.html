An engine room leading hand and the child of Gilbert Danton (a gas stoker) and Sarah Tutt, Henry Danton, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Margate, Kent, England on 6 Dec 18821,2,3,4 and married Clara Winters (with whom he had 1 child, Katherine) in New Zealand in 1913.

During his life, he was living at Ledbury Street, Peckham, London, England on 5 Apr 18915; at 10 Lochnagar Street, Poplar, London on 1 Nov 1916; and at 2/3 British Sailors Society, Orchard Place, Southampton, Hampshire, England on 29 Sept 19391. He served in the army from 1914 to 1920 (soldier Number: 949, Rank: Corporal, Corps: Royal Field Artillery).

He died c. Nov 1955 in Poplar, London, England4.
