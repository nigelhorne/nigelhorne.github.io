Ann Stuple (née Mount) c1804 - 1882

The child of Thomas Mount (an agricultural labourer) and Mary Buxey, Ann Mount, the 3 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Sturry, Kent, England c. 18041,2,3,4,5 and married Isaac Stuple (with whom she had 5 children: Abraham, Thomas, Jane, James and Emily) in Herne, Kent, England on 13 Oct 1830.

During her life, she was living in Herne in 18511 (the same place as her mother had been living in 1797), in 18612 (the same place as her father had been living in 1851) and in 18713; and Blean Union Workhouse in Herne on 3 Apr 18814 which is where she died in 18825 (oct/Nov/Dec).
