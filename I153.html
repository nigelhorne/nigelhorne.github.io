Ann Stuple (née Mount) c1804 - 1882

The child of Thomas Mount (an agricultural laborer) and Mary Buxey, Ann Mount, the 3 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Sturry, Kent, England c. 18041,2,3,4,5 and married Isaac Stuple (with whom she had 5 children: Abraham, Thomas, Jane, James and Emily) in Herne, Kent, England on Oct 13, 1830.

During her life, she was living in Herne in 18511, in 18612 and in 18713; and Blean Union Workhouse in Herne on Apr 3, 18814 which is where she died in 18825 (oct/Nov/Dec).
