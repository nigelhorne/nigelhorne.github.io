Thomas Gurney c1713 - 1771

Thomas Gurney, the husband of Jane Cowell (the six times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born c. 17131 and baptised in Kent, England on 26 Feb 1713. He married Jane at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent on 29 May 17321, which is also where he died in 1771.
