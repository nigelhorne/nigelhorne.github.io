Thomas Gurney c1713 - 1771

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Thomas Gurney, the husband of Jane Cowell (the six times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born c. 17131 and baptized in Kent, England on Feb 26, 1713. He married Jane at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent on May 29, 17321, which is also where he died in 1771.
