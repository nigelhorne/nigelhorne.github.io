Thomas Gurney, the husband of Jane Cowell (the six times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born <i>c.</i> 1713<span class="citation">1</span> and baptized in Kent, England on Feb 26, 1713. He married Jane at St John the Baptist Church, Margate, Kent on May 29, 1732<span class="citation">1</span>, which is also where he died in 1771.
