The child of James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company) and Emily Lawrence, Dorothy Cowell, (also known as Dolly Cowell) the aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Sutton, Kent, England on 14 Nov 19171,2,3,4,5 and married Henry Pearson (with whom she had 2 children: Helen Anne and Jill, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mary's Church, Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on 21 Oct 19396.

During her life, she was living at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on 19 Jun 19211 and on 29 Sept 19392; and at 3 Laundry Road, Minster in Thanet in 19657.

She died on 14 Jan 2003 in Herne Bay, Kent, England5.
