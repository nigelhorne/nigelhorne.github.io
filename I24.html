The child of James Cowell (a seaman collier with the whitstable shipping company) and Emily Lawrence, Dorothy Cowell, (also known as Dolly Cowell) the aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Sutton, Kent, England on Nov 14, 19171,2,3,4,5 and married Henry Pearson (with whom she had 2 children: Helen Anne and Jill, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mary's Church, Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on Oct 21, 19396.

During her life, she was living at 7 Pouces Cottages, Minster in Thanet on Jun 19, 19211 and on Sep 29, 19392; and at 3 Laundry Road, Minster in Thanet in 19657.

She died on Jan 14, 2003 in Herne Bay, Kent, England5.
