A house boy on a farm and the child of William Fordham and Mary Baker, John Fordham was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England <i>c.</i> Nov 1887<span class="citation">1,2,3,4,5,6,7</span> and married Maria Rowland (with whom he had 1 child, Rosa Estelle) in Thanet, Kent, England in Jan/feb/mar 1912<span class="citation">11</span>.</p><p>Throughout his life, John lived in several places: St Peters in Thanet in 1891<span class="citation">1</span>; at Reading Street, St Peters in Thanet on Apr 5, 1891<span class="citation">12</span>; in Staple, Kent on Mar 31, 1901<span class="citation">13</span>; and St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 1911<span class="citation">3</span> and in 1915<span class="citation">7</span>.
