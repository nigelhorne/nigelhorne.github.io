The daughter of Charles Horne (a market gardener worker) and Amy SprattIda Horne, the first cousin once-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Haine, Thanet, Kent, England on Nov 20, 19101,2,3.

Ida spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: at Walnut Tree Cottages, Haine, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 2, 19116; at Sacketts Hill Farm, St Peters in Thanet on Jun 19, 19217; on Refectory House, Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet on Sep 29, 19392; and at 18 Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet in 1955 (when she was living with her father, Charles Henry).

She died on Oct 31, 1955 in Margate General Hospital, Margate, Kent4,5.
