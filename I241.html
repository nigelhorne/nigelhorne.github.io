James Tutt 1851 - 1915

A general laborer and the son of Edmund Tutt (a sawyer) and Caroline Page (a laundress)James Tutt, the great-great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Dover, Kent, England on Jan 16, 18511,2,3,4,5,6,7,8 and married Jane Foord (a general shop with whom he had 8 children: Jane Eliza Caroline, James Richard Edmund, Jessie Maria, Edmund Thomas, Lily, Harriet, Rose Elizabeth and Frank, along with 1 surviving child) at St Lawrence Parish Church, St Lawrence, Thanet, Kent, England on Apr 29, 18769 (KFHS CD 29).

James spent all of his life in Kent, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at 40 George Street, Buckland on Mar 30, 185115, when he was living with his sisters, Jane and Eliza; at 13 Shooter's Hill, Buckland on Apr 7, 186114, when he was living with his sister, Jane; at 18 Clarence Terrace, Ramsgate on Apr 3, 188113; on 10 Clarence Road, Ramsgate on Apr 5, 189110; on 8 Hillbrow Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Mar 31, 190111 - less than a mile from his nephew Charles Henry Horne, nephew Edmund Horne and sister Jane Tutt who were living at 15 Haine Cottages, St Lawrence in Thanet; and on 7 Hillbrow Road, St Lawrence in Thanet on Apr 2, 191112 which is where he died on Dec 18, 19158 ((1) Asthma Chronic 20 years; (2) Bronchitis Catarrhal).
