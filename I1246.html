Sidney Charles Hammond 1881 - 1917

A marine steward and the son of John Hammond and Mary Standing (a lodge house keeper)Sidney Hammond, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Folkestone, Kent, England on Jun 24, 18811,2,3,4,5,6.

Throughout his life, he lived at 4 Richmond Street in Folkestone on Apr 5, 189112, when he was living with his sister, Jessie Elizabeth and on Mar 31, 190113. During 1917, he was serving in the Army in Western European Theatre (regiment: Queen's Own (Royal West Kent Regiment); Rank: Private; Service number: G/5001).

He died on Oct 4, 1917 in Passchendaele, Belgium5,6,7,8,9,10,11 and was buried at Zonnebeke, Ypres, West Flanders, Belgium after Oct 4, 19178.
