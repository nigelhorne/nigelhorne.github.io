The older of 2 children of Charles Cowell (a lorry driver) and Edith Bailey, Trevor Cowell, a cousin on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Thanet, Kent, England on 8 Jun 19381,2,3,4 and. He married Muriel Knox (with whom he had 2 surviving children Charles J and Andrew J) in Thanet around Aug 19595.
