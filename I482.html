Catherine Fox (née Newport) c1784 - 1865

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

The child of John Newport and Susanna Brooks, Catherine Newport, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Upper Hardres, Kent, England c. 17841,2,3 and baptised in Ham, Kent, England on 19 Sept 1784. She married William Fox (a farming 10 acres employing 3 men) on St Mary the Virgin, Vicarage Lane, Elham, Kent, England on 26 Nov 18037.

During her life, she was living in Sibertswold, Kent, England in 18411; at Butter Street in Sibertswold on 30 Mar 18519; and in Sibertswold in 18613 (the same place as her mother had been living in 1841).

She died on 15 May 1865 in Shepherdswell, Kent, England4,5,6 and was buried in Sibertswold on 19 May 18658.
