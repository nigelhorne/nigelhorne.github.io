Catherine Fox (née Newport) c1784 - 1865

The child of John Newport and Susanna Brooks, Catherine Newport, the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Upper Hardres, Kent, England c. 17841,2,3 and baptized in Ham, Kent, England on Sep 19, 1784. She married William Fox (a farming 10 acres employing 3 men) on St Mary the Virgin, Vicarage Lane, Elham, Kent, England on Nov 26, 18037.

During her life, she was living in Sibertswold, Kent, England in 18411; at Butter Street in Sibertswold on Mar 30, 18519; and in Sibertswold in 18613.

She died on May 15, 1865 in Shepherdswell, Kent, England4,5,6 and was buried in Sibertswold on May 19, 18658.
