The child of Richard Neame and Susanna, Richard Neame, the 9 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in Woodnesborough, Kent, England in 1623, and was orphaned at the age of 13 following the death of his father in 1623 and mother in 1636.

He married Mary Denne (with whom he had 1 child, John) in Kent, England in 1652

He died c. 17041 and was buried in Woodnesborough on 21 Mar 17041.
