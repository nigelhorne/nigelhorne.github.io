William Strand, the husband of Anna Maria Poysden (the four times great-aunt of Nigel Horne), was born in Chislet, Kent, England in 18041,2,3,4,5,6 and married Anna (with whom he had 4 children: Eliza, William, Joseph Thomas and Edwin, along with 2 surviving children) in Sturry, Kent, England on Jun 1, 18338.
