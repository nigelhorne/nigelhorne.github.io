William Culmer c1815 - 1896

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of John Culmer (a parish clerk) and Sarah HawkesWilliam Culmer, the four times great-uncle of Nigel Horne, was born in Stourmouth, Kent, England c. 18151,2,3,4,5,6 and baptised there at All Saints Church, Church Lane on 11 Jun 1815. He was married to Elizabeth.

William spent all of his life in Kent in England. Throughout his life, he lived in Sholden, Kent, England in 18511, in 18612 and in 18713; and in Deal, Kent, England in 18814 and in 18915.

He died in Jan/Feb/Mar 1896 in Eastry, Kent, England6.
