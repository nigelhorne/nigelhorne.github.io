The daughter of William Adamson and Mary FlattMartha Adamson was born in North Shields, Northumberland, England on Mar 22, 18551,2,3 and married Henry Myers (a steamboat man with whom she had 10 children: Robert, Mary Ann Nicholson, Thomasina B Nicholson, Martha Sarah Nicholson, Henrietta Briscoe Nicholson, Susanna Cranmer, Lavinia Clements, Christina Robertson, Wilhelmina White Ridley Adamson and Isabella Jane Nicholson, along with 1 surviving child) at St Peters Church, Tynemouth, Tyne and Wear, England on Jan 12, 18744.

Throughout her life, Martha lived in several places: at 2 South Street, North Shields, Tyne and Wear on Apr 3, 18816; at 16 Appleby Street, North Shields, Tyne and Wear on Apr 5, 18915; and at 48 Appleby Street, North Shields, Tyne and Wear on Mar 31, 19011.

She died on Mar 11, 1910 in Tynemouth3.
