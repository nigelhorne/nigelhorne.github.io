Martha Sarah Nicholson Wood (née Myers) 1883 - 1974

The child of Henry Myers (a steamboat man) and Martha Adamson, Martha Myers was born in Sunderland, Durham, England on 27 Jun 18831,2,3 and married William Wood (a plasterer with whom she had 5 children: William Henry, Martha Sarah, James, Hilda and Wilhelmina, along with 1 surviving child) in Tynemouth, Tyne and Wear, England around Nov 19074.

During her life, she was living at 16 Appleby Street, Chirton, Tyne and Wear on 5 Apr 18915; at 48 Appleby Street, Chirton, Tyne and Wear on 31 Mar 19016; at 40 Burdon Main Row in Tynemouth on 2 Apr 19117; and at 3 Langley Road in Tynemouth on 29 Sept 19391 - less than a mile from her niece Hilda Wood who was living at 2 Langley Road in Tynemouth which is where she died on 21 Oct 19743. She was buried at Preston Cemetery, Walton Avenue, North Shields, Tyne and Wear after 21 Oct 1974.
