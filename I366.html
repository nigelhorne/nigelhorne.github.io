Mary Elizabeth Lawrence (née Knowler) 1896 - 1979

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The daughter of Thomas Knowler (an agricultural laborer) and Jane LawrenceMary Knowler, the first cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Eastry, Kent, England on Jul 26, 18961,2,3,4,5 and married her cousin on her mother's side James Lawrence (a general farm worker with whom she had 5 children: James Thomas, George Edward, Thomas William, Jack and Ronald) at St Augustine, Northbourne, Kent, England on Sep 14, 19187 (JulAug/Sep).

Mary spent all of her life in Kent, England. Throughout her life, she lived in several places around the county: in Woodnesborough, Kent, England in 19011; at Meadow Cottages, Marshside on Jun 19, 19218; at 1 Church Cottage in Woodnesborough on Sep 29, 19392, when she was living with her son Ronald, following the death of her husband on Jan 20, 1956; at 8 Fitzroy Avenue, Ramsgate in 1965; and at 3 Payton Close, Margate in 1979.

She died on Jan 17, 1979 in Thanet, Kent, England4,6.
