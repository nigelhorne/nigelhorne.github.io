Esther Dale (née Poysden) 1825 - 1902

[ Home ] | [ Calendar ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Errors ] | [ Family History ]

A servant and the child of George Poysden (an agricultural labourer) and Sophia Chapple, Esther Poysden, the great-great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Canterbury, Kent, England in 18251,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10, was baptised in Blean, Kent, England on 27 Jun 1825 and married George Dale (an agricultural labourer with whom she had 9 children: George, Mary, Sophia, Sarah Ann, Edward, Esther Louisa, William Thomas, Henry and Leonard, along with 1 surviving child) at St Mary's Church, Chartham, Kent, England on 13 Oct 184911 (Chartham Parish Church).

During her life, she was living in Chartham in 18411 and in 18512; in Chartham Hatch, Kent, England on 7 Apr 186112 (the same place as her parents had been living on 30 Mar 1851), on 2 Apr 187114 and on 3 Apr 18816; in Chartham on 5 Apr 18917 following the death of her husband on 3 Feb 1891; and at 88 Church Hill, Temple Ewell, Kent, England on 31 Mar 19018.

She died on 28 Feb 1902 Church Hill in Temple Ewell9 (influenza, pneumonia, pleurisy) and was buried in Temple Ewell on 6 Mar 1902.
