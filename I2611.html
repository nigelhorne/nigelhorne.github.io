The child of John Carley (a blacksmith) and Ann Boodle, Levi Carley, the second cousin twice-removed on the mother's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Biddenden, Kent, England on 19 Feb 18801,2, was baptised in Wye, Kent, England on 29 Aug 1880 and married Rosamund Flaxman (with whom he had 10 children: Daphne, Gwen, Phyllis, Leslie John, Hilda Dorothy, John William, Gordon, Muriel, Harold and Richard, along with 4 surviving children) in Calgary, Alberta, Canada in 1909. On 3 Apr 1881, he was living at Church Farm Cottage, Harty, Kent, England1. He arrived in Canada in 1900.

He died on 20 Aug 1969 at Stensrud Lodge, Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada and was buried at Woodlawn Cemetery, Saskatoon after 20 Aug 1969.
