A police constable and the son of Thomas Horne (a brewer's drayman) and Harriett Woods, John Horne, the first cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in St Pancras, London, England on Feb 18, 18861,2,3, was baptized there on Apr 21, 1886 and married Mary Stitson (with whom he had 1 child, Winifred Mary) on St Jude's Church, Gray's Inn Road, Camden, London, England on Apr 18, 19124.

John spent all of his life in London, England. Throughout his life, he lived in several places around the county: at Britannia Street in St Pancras on Mar 31, 19017; at Police Station, Hyde Park on Apr 2, 19118; at 94 Scott Ellis Gardens, St John's Wood on Jun 14, 19146; on 19 Grittleton Road, Maida Vale on Sep 29, 19391, when he was living with his sisters, Rosa Emily and Hilda Alice; and in at 221 Elgin Avenue in 1973.

He died on Sep 30, 1973 in Paddington, London3 and was buried in Camden on Oct 5, 19735.
