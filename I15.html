A nurse and the 3rd of 7 children of Isaac Horne (a hay trusser & thatcher heavy worker market gardener) and May Spratt, Gladys Horne, the aunt of Nigel Horne, was born in Ramsgate, Kent, England on 25 Jan 19141,2,3,4.

During her life, she was living at Pansy Cottage, Way, Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on 19 Jun 19211; at 68 Lynton Avenue, Ealing, London, England in 19359; at 55 Salisbury Road, Banstead, Surrey, England on 29 Sept 19392; and at 53 Bassetts Way, Farnborough, Kent, England in 1958. In 1938 she travelled from Malta to London, England, arriving on 16 Dec.

She died on 11 Jul 1958 in Farnborough Hospital, Orpington, Kent5,6,7 and was buried on Minster Cemetery, Tothill Street, Minster in Thanet in 19588.
