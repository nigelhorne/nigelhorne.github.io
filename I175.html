Thomas Mount 1774 - 1851

An agricultural labourer and the child of John Mount and Margaret Lamb, Thomas Mount, the 4 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England in 17741,2,3, was baptised there on 18 Dec 1774 and married Mary Buxey (with whom he had 3 children: Elizabeth, Ann and Thomas) in Herne, Kent, England on 4 Nov 17975 (archiver.rootsweb.com KFHS CD21).

During his life, he was living in Herne in 18517 and at Blean Union Workhouse in Herne on 30 Mar 18518.

He died on 23 Aug 1851 Blean Union Workhouse in Herne4 and was buried at St Martin in Herne on 26 Aug 18516.
