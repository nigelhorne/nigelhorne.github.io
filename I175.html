Thomas Mount 1774 - 1851

An agricultural laborer and the child of John Mount and Margaret Lamb, Thomas Mount, the 4 times great-grandfather of Nigel Horne, was born in St Nicholas-at-Wade, Kent, England in 17741,2,3, was baptized there on Dec 18, 1774 and married Mary Buxey (with whom he had 3 children: Elizabeth, Ann and Thomas) in Herne, Kent, England on Nov 4, 17975 (archiver.rootsweb.com KFHS CD21).

During his life, he was living in Herne in 18517; and at Blean Union Workhouse in Herne on Mar 30, 18518.

He died on Aug 23, 1851 Blean Union Workhouse in Herne4 and was buried at St Martin in Herne on Aug 26, 18516.
