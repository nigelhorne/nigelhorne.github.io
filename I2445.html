A laborer and the child of Thomas Mount (an agricultural laborer) and Mary Buxey, Thomas Mount, the four times great-uncle of <a href="I1.html">Nigel Horne</a>, was born in Sturry, Kent, England <i>c.</i> 1814<span class="citation">1</span>, was baptized there at St Nicholas' Church on Mar 13, 1814 and married Sarah Holness (a charwoman with whom he had 10 children: <a href="I3427.html">Jane</a>, <a href="I3414.html">Mary</a>, <a href="I3428.html">Thomas</a>, <a href="I3415.html">Elizabeth</a>, <a href="I3416.html">William</a>, <a href="I2453.html">Caroline</a>, <a href="I2657.html">James</a>, <a href="I3417.html">Alfred</a>, <a href="I3418.html">Sarah Ann</a> and <a href="I3419.html">Alice</a>) at St Martin's Church, Herne, Kent, England on Jul 14, 1838<span class="citation">3</span>.</p><p>Thomas spent all of his life in Kent, England.
