Lydia Florence Pierce (née Bonner) 1915 - 1988

Lydia Bonner, the wife of James Sidney Cowell (the uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Brighton, Sussex, England on Sep 10, 1915 and was married twice - to James Cowell (on Sep 10, 1938 in Margate, Kent, England) George Pierce (on Jul 31, 1954 in Edmonton, London, England)1. She had 3 children with James Cowell: Ronald J, James Roger and Wendy J.

Throughout her life, she lived on 3 Church Road in Margate on Sep 29, 1939; and at Flat 2, 156 High Street in Margate in 1988.

She died on Jul 17, 1988 at Ramsgate Hospital, Ramsgate, Kent (cerebrovascular incident) and was buried at Margate Cemetery in Margate after Jul 17, 1988.
