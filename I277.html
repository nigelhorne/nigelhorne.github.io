Lydia Bonner, the wife of James Sidney Cowell (the uncle of Nigel Horne), was born in Brighton, Sussex, England on 10 Sept 1915 and was married twice - to James Cowell (on 10 Sept 1938 in Margate, Kent, England) George Pierce (on 31 Jul 1954 in Edmonton, London, England)1. She had 3 children with James Cowell: Ronald J, James Roger and Wendy J.

During her life, she was living at 3 Church Road in Margate on 29 Sept 1939; and at Flat 2, 156 High Street in Margate in 1988.

She died on 17 Jul 1988 at Ramsgate Hospital, Ramsgate, Kent (cerebrovascular incident) and was buried at Margate Cemetery in Margate after 17 Jul 1988.
