Sarah Chandler (née Crow) 1791 - 1878

The youngest of 3 children of William Crow and Elizabeth Hewitt, Sarah Crow, the 4 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born in Minster, Thanet, Kent, England on 11 Mar 17911,2,3,4, was baptised there at St Mary the Virgin on 13 Mar 1791 was married twice - to Thomas Brooker (on 14 Jul 1811 in Minster) James Chandler (on 31 Aug 1816 in Minster, following the death of Thomas in 1814). She had 2 children, both with Thomas Brooker: Johanna and Mary.

During her life, she was living at her birthplace in 18411, in 18512 and in 18613 which is where she died in 18784. She was buried there at St Mary the Virgin on 2 Apr 1878.
