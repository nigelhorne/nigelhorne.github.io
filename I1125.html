The son of Frederick Peters (a miller) and Celia ColpittsFrederick Peters, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Peters Mills, Kent, New Brunswick, Canada on Jan 23, 18881,2,3,4,5.

Throughout his life, Frederick lived in several places: in Richibucto, Kent, New Brunswick, Canada in 18911 and in 19012; in Kent in 19113 (the same place as his father had been living in 1881); and in Canada in 19146 and in 19154.

He died on Oct 1, 1916 in Rouen, Normandy, France5,6,7 (gunshot wounds to the left arm and scalp.) and was buried there at St. Sever Cemetery after Oct 1, 19165,6,7.
