The child of Frederick Peters (a miller) and Celia Colpitts, Frederick Peters, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Peters Mills, Kent, New Brunswick, Canada on 23 Jan 18881,2,3,4,5.

During his life, he was living in Richibucto, Kent, New Brunswick, Canada in 18911 and in 19012; in Kent in 19113 (the same place as his father had been living in 1881); and in Canada in 19146 and in 19154.

He died on 1 Oct 1916 in Rouen, Normandy, France5,6,7 (gunshot wounds to the left arm and scalp.) and was buried there at St. Sever Cemetery after 1 Oct 19165,6,7.
