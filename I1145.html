The youngest of 7 children of Richmond Peters (a farmer) and Caroline Warman, Eunice Peters, the third cousin twice-removed on the father's side of Nigel Horne, was born in Barkers Point, York, New Brunswick, Canada on 10 Sept 18971,2,3.

During her life, she was living in New Brunswick, Canada in 19015; in York, New Brunswick, Canada in 19111; in Douglas, New Brunswick on 1 Jun 19212; and in York in 19454 (the same place as her mother had been living in 1911). In 1927 she travelled from Hong Kong, China to Victoria, British Columbia, Canada, arriving 27 Mar.
