The child of William Belsey (a labourer) and Anne, Dorcas Belsey, the 3 times great-grandmother of Nigel Horne, was born c. 1813, was baptised in Ickham, Kent, England on 12 Sept 1813 and married George Colyer (an agricultural labourer with whom she had 1 child, Mary Ann) at Ss Cosmus & Damian, Blean, Kent, England on 23 Mar 18332.

She died c. 1835 in Kent1 and was buried in Hackington, Kent, England on 7 Apr 1835 (age 22 on burial record)1.
